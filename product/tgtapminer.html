ABOUT GAME

Game Informations

BlockDAG TG Tap Miner is a simple and rewarding game available right in your Telegram app.

Just open the game, start tapping, and earn points that you can convert into BDAG coins. Game sessions are available every 2 hours, giving you plenty of chances to maximize your rewards throughout the day.

You can boost your earnings by purchasing BDAGs during the Presale, unlocking special boxes and boosters to enhance your gameplay. Complete tasks to earn even more rewards, and invite your friends to join for additional bonuses. Compete against friends on the leaderboard and see who can tap their way to the top.

No need to download anything—just tap, earn, and enjoy your rewards directly in Telegram.
