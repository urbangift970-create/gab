Step 3

Once you’ve selected your miners and confirmed the quantity, proceed to the payment.

If paying with ETH, BNB, or USDT, your wallet will automatically process the transaction when you click "Buy Miners." Just ensure you have sufficient funds. Buy ETH, BNB & USDT with Apple Pay, Google Pay or Card

  • Visit https://www.moonpay.com/buy to purchase ETH, BNB, or USDT.

  • The purchased cryptocurrency will be sent directly to your wallet.

  • You can then use this ETH, BNB, or USDT to purchase BDAG Coins.

How to Buy Miners (For ETH, USDT, and BNB)

  • Go to the BlockDAG Network website, choose the miner you wish to purchase, and enter the quantity.

  • Choose ETH, USDT, or BNB as your preferred payment option.

  • Ensure that your wallet has enough funds to cover the purchase.

  • Once your wallet is connected and the currency is selected, click on “Buy Miners.” The purchase will be processed immediately.

  • Since your wallet is linked, payment is made directly, and your transaction will be confirmed automatically.

  • Make sure your delivery address is accurate.

How to Buy Miners with Other Cryptos

  • Ensure your wallet is linked to the BlockDAG Network and you're on the correct crypto network.

  • Choose your preferred cryptocurrency from the available options (e.g., BTC, USDT, KAS, DOGE).

  • Ensure you have sufficient balance in your wallet to complete the transaction.

  • Click on “Buy Miners” and confirm your selection.

  • An order summary will appear. Note down the Order Number for future reference.

  • A QR code and wallet address will be displayed. Send the exact amount of cryptocurrency to the provided wallet address.

  • Keep an eye on the payment status. It will update once your transaction is confirmed.

  • Make sure your delivery address is accurate.

Step 4

Before completing your purchase, take a moment to verify your wallet connection and miner selection. Make sure everything is correct before confirming your order.
