What is
Staking?

Staking is a way to earn rewards by putting your crypto to work on a blockchain network. In return for helping the network run smoothly and securely, you receive more of the BDAG you're staking. The rewards come from the network itself, your crypto isn’t being lent out. It’s a safe, simple and popular way to grow your crypto while holding.

  • Staking is a way to earn rewards on your BDAG by putting it to work — all you need to do is click ‘stake’ on BlockDAG
  • Staking is secure, and does not involve lending out your crypto. You earn rewards for helping the blockchain run smoothly
  • Before staking your assets, make sure you understand the specifics of the network, such as wait times to start earning or unstake
