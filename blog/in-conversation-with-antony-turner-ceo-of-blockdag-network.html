In Conversation with Antony Turner, CEO of BlockDAG Network

Release Date: July 31, 2024

  • Antony Turner on BlockDAG’s Vision:

  • The BlockDAG Journey

  • The Team and Vision

  • The BlockDAG Vision & Goals

Hello, blog community! Hold on tight because the BlockDAG Network is causing a storm, and we’re about to take you straight to the heart of our revolutionary-tornado. Yesterday, we set the stage ablaze with the big reveal of our superstar CEO Antony Turner, our executive dream team, and a powerhouse advisory board. The response? Mind-blowing! Our community and growth have exploded overnight, and we couldn’t be more thrilled. But guess what? The party is just getting started!

 

Today, we’re turning up the heat even more with a live AMA (ask me anything) event on our official Telegram channel at 1pm PST. Yes, you read that right! Antony Turner himself, along with our all-star team, will be diving deep into what makes BlockDAG Network the crème de la crème of the digital world. So, set those reminders and don’t miss out on the chance to interact with the brains behind the magic. This is your golden ticket to witness our greatness live!

 

Now, before the AMA frenzy begins, we snagged a one-on-one with our CEO Antony Turner. Picture this: a cozy, laid-back chat, like sipping coffee with a friend, but this friend happens to be steering the ship of the most innovative blockchain project out there.

 

Here’s how it went….



 

Antony Turner on BlockDAG’s Vision:

Let’s start by saying that Antony is like the captain of a high-tech ship, sailing through the digital seas, charting new territories, and discovering treasures in the world of decentralized technology. He shared some personal anecdotes, recalling his early days when the idea of BlockDAG was just a spark in his mind. “Imagine being in a dark forest with just a lantern,” he said, “BlockDAG is the light that guides us through the unknown, illuminating new paths and possibilities.” And boy, has it been a journey! From sleepless nights to breakthrough and proud moments, Antony’s passion is palpable, and it’s contagious.

 

1. Can you tell us about your journey into the crypto and blockchain industry?

 

Antony’s entry into the crypto world wasn’t, regretfully, during Bitcoin’s infancy, but it’s been a thrilling ride nonetheless. He co-founded SwissOne Capital, a first-of-its-kind crypto index fund, right before the chilly crypto winter of 2019. Despite the cold start, the fund is still thriving, proving that perseverance pays off. From there, Antony moved to a digital assets investment firm, helping it list on the Canadian Securities Exchange. It’s like he’s been surfing the waves of the crypto world, riding each high and low with finesse and never giving up!

 

2. What unique skills and perspectives do you bring to the BlockDAG Network?

 

Antony says he sees himself as the coach of a top-tier sports team, building the best roster, planning strategies, and tackling challenges head-on. He believes in balancing the new and the traditional, blending blockchain’s cutting-edge potential with the reliability of conventional finance. It’s like mixing a classic recipe with modern twists to create something new but that everyone wants a bite of!

 

3. What motivated you to create BlockDAG Network, and what vision did you have for it?

 

With so many blockchains out there, Antony saw an opportunity to synthesize the best innovations into one powerhouse network. Think of BlockDAG as the ultimate melting-pot, featuring the best tech from multiple sources. He’s all about speed, decentralization, and efficiency — key ingredients that make BlockDAG the blockchain world’s equivalent of a high-performance sports car.



 

The BlockDAG Journey

How has your previous experience prepared you for leading BlockDAG Network?

 

With 30 years of experience across various digital industries, Antony has a treasure trove of knowledge. From Fintech to education and beyond, he’s seen it all — both successes and failures. His takeaway? Perseverance is key.And yes, he’s got some serious coding chops too, making him a well-rounded leader who understands every aspect of the business.

 

What do you consider to be your greatest achievement in your career at BlockDAG so far?

 

Antony humbly shares that the team’s greatest achievement has been the overwhelming support from their community. With tens of thousands of people backing the project, and over 100k followers on Twitter, and raising more than $62 million in presale (with a 40% price increase imminent), it’s clear BlockDAG Network has reached “global phenomenon” status.

 

What challenges have you faced while building BlockDAG Network, and how did you overcome them?

 

Criticism and skepticism have been hurdles, but the team is tackling them by being transparent and engaging with the community. Regular AMAs and clear marketing materials are part of our strategy to build trust and understanding and an even bigger global community.

The Team and Vision

How did you assemble the team, and what are their key strengths?

 

For Antony, building a team is about more than just skills; it’s about attitude, resilience, and the ability to work together. Our BlockDAG team, he says, is a diverse group, each bringing something unique to the table, making them an unbeatable force in this very competitive industry.

 

What is your leadership style, and how do you inspire your team to achieve their best?

 

Antony leads with a light touch, preferring to encourage and guide rather than micromanage. He believes in leading by example and fostering a positive, democratic work environment.

 

What message or advice would you give to aspiring BlockDAG users looking to enter the space?

 

Don’t wait! The crypto world is bursting with opportunities. Antony’s advice is to try getting into a project early, if you find the one that suits your investment needs and sparks your interest that is. Early Bitcoin adopters know how important it is to start believing in something that has the potential to go out and reshape the industry!



 

The BlockDAG Vision & Goals

What is the core vision behind BlockDAG Network?

 

BlockDAG aims to create a blockchain that overcomes the limitations of current systems and that can challenge Bitcoin and Kaspa. BlockDAG Network focuses on speed, decentralization, transparency and efficiency.With BlockDAG, it’s all about pushing the boundaries of what blockchain technology can achieve.

 

How does BlockDAG Network differentiate itself from other blockchain projects out there?

 

BlockDAG enhances the Proof of Work model with innovative features for energy efficiency, scalability, and fairness. Think of it as Bitcoin on steroids but with a low-cost twist.

 

What are the short-term and long-term goals of BlockDAG Network?

 

In the short term, BlockDAG aims to complete its token pre-sale and launch on the mainnet. Long-term goals include fostering a vibrant developer community and setting up a foundation to manage resources sustainably.

 

What Makes BlockDAG the Best in the Biz?

 

Antony didn’t hold back on why BlockDAG Network stands out. There are many ways in which BlockDAG stands out but most of all Antony emphasized the importance of community engagement and innovation, saying:

 

“We’re not just building a network; we’re building a movement. It’s about creating a space where every voice matters and every idea can spark change.”



 

Antony also hinted at some jaw-dropping projects in the pipeline and has invited everyone to stay tuned as there’s so much more to explore. The excitement is palpable, and the team’s enthusiasm is infectious. We’re on the brink of something extraordinary, and we want you all to be a part of this journey!

 

So, blog readers, that’s a wrap on this insightful chat with Antony Turner! Now gear up for the AMA, and let’s make some noise! Tune in, ask your burning questions, and get to know the masterminds behind BlockDAG. We’re just getting started, and the best is yet to come. Stay connected with us through our official social media channels for more updates, more reveals, and more #BlockDAGBOOMS because we’re just warming up!

 

Catch you in our next article, and don’t forget to stay tuned for all things BlockDAG!
