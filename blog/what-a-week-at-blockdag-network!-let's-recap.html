What a Week at BlockDAG Network! Let’s Recap

Release Date: August 2, 2024

  • The BlockDAG Week in Review: No More “Gatekeeping”

  • Why Now is the Perfect Time to Invest

  • The #BlockDAGPhenomenon

  • The Future is Bright

Hey BlockDAG community! Welcome back to another blog article, wrapping up an explosive week here at BlockDAG Network full of milestones reached, updates, and investment opportunities. Buckle up, because we’re about to dive into the whirlwind of news and updates that have rocked our world since Monday. Trust us, you won’t want to miss this!

The BlockDAG Week in Review: No More “Gatekeeping”

Monday: We kicked off the week with a bang, unveiling our brilliant CEO, Anthony Turner, along with our stellar Executive Team and Advisory Board.The excitement was real, and it set the tone for what has become our best week ever! Anthony’s extensive experience with projects such as SwissOne Capital and Spirit Blockchain provided his new venture, BlockDAG, a solid foundation for our future growth. We also introduced key team members like Steven, who handles policy and partnerships, Youssef, our tech wizard, and Maurice Herlihy, a blockchain and cryptography expert from Brown University. Their combined expertise is steering BlockDAG towards a bright future.

 

Tuesday: Our first-ever live AMA event was a resounding success! Anthony and the team answered your burning questions, shared our vision, and connected with our fantastic global community. The AMA was a no-frills, straight-from-the-heart session where we acknowledged past missteps, like not introducing the team sooner, and committed to full transparency moving forward. We were thrilled to reveal that we now have over 100,000 unique coin holders from more than 150 countries, and we’re on track to surpass 1 million holders by the end of our presale. This level of community support will catapult us into the top 50 coins by market cap.

 

Wednesday: Major updates rolled out for the BlockDAG X1 Miner App. We hit a staggering milestone of over 100,000 active users! This is a testament to our app’s popularity and usability. The new features include:

 

- Notification Module: Get real-time updates for mining sessions and referral statuses, keeping you in the loop at all times.

 

- Referral Module: Added a third status symbol for inactive states, improved network connectivity indicators, and implemented pagination for a smoother user experience.

 

- User Interface Improvements: Enjoy pull-to-refresh functionality in the leaderboard and referral module, making navigation more intuitive.

 

- User Onboarding Module: Enhanced the OTP screen with a better timer and OTP send functionality for a seamless onboarding process.

 

- Balance Display: For added security, balances are now hidden with a * symbol.

 

These updates are now live on Apple App Store, Huawei App Gallery, Amazon App Store, and available for APK Direct Download. We’re continuously working to improve the app based on user feedback, ensuring it meets the highest standards of performance and security.

 

Thursday: Our presale hit a phenomenal milestone — over $64 million raised, and a 40% price increase reached! This is a massive vote of confidence from our community and investors. It’s clear that people see the potential and promise of BlockDAG Network. This surge in investment and global adoption really reflects the trust and excitement surrounding our project.

Why Now is the Perfect Time to Invest

Given the current uncertainty in the crypto market, especially with Bitcoin fluctuating between $63k and $64k, investing in our presale is a strategic move. Here’s why:

 

1. Store of Value: BDAG offers a stable store of value with significant ROI potential. In a market where traditional assets might be volatile, BDAG, in presale, might just be a more reliable store of value until the crypto waters calm and the market is bullish again.

 

2. Market Conditions: With the market’s uncertainty expected to continue until after the US elections, BDAG could provide a safer investment alternative. Political and economic factors can greatly impact crypto markets, and having a more “stable” investment like BDAG can provide peace of mind.

 

3. Maximize Returns: At current Bitcoin prices, you can get more BDAGs for your buck, enhancing your potential returns. Investing now means that you are advantageously positioned before the bullish market is back.

The #BlockDAGPhenomenon

The BlockDAG Phenomenon is real, and it’s growing stronger every day. In just six months, BlockDAG has gone from being an underdog to a global sensation. Here’s a snapshot of how we went from anonymity to global recognition:

 

Global Marketing Impact: Our marketing campaigns have left an indelible mark in some of the world’s biggest cities, including Tokyo, New York, and London. These campaigns weren’t just billboards on shiny screens — they were statements, signaling our entry into the global arena. We’ve captured the attention of both crypto enthusiasts and the general public.

 

High-Profile Sponsorships: We’ve secured sponsorship from a world champion UFC boxer, Alex Pereira aka Poatan with more exciting sponsors lined up. Yes, you read that right — this is just the beginning of our star-studded journey! These partnerships enhance our visibility and credibility in the market.

 

Media Buzz: Major publications worldwide are talking about us. From tech blogs to global financial news, BlockDAG is the name on everyone’s lips.

 

Innovative Technology: We didn’t just stop at marketing. We launched a groundbreaking “on the go” mining app the BlockDAG X1 Mining App, allowing users to mine on the go and earn passive income from anywhere, anytime. Our app’s user-friendly interface and robust features have made it a hit among users. Major publications worldwide have covered our journey, highlighting our rapid ascent in the crypto world.

 

Community Trust: We’ve also created a safe and transparent environment, building trust with our growing community. Every day, we climb higher on the crypto mountain, setting new standards and trends. Transparency is at the core of our operations, and we’re committed to keeping our community informed and engaged and end “gatekeeping” in crypto.

The Future is Bright

The BlockDAG Phenomenon is growing stronger each day. With our recent team reveal and a renewed commitment to transparency, right now, as the predictions and analysis show, we’re poised to soar even higher than we were a week ago. Our journey is just beginning, and the only direction we’re heading is up. This is a golden opportunity you don’t want to miss!



 

Stay tuned for more updates, keep following, sharing, and believing in BlockDAG. Together, we’re redefining the future of blockchain!
