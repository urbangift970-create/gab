Best New Crypto to Buy in 2025 for Higher Returns

Release Date: January 13, 2025

  • BlockDAG (BDAG)

  • Ethereum (ETH)

  • Ripple (XRP)

  • Dogecoin (DOGE)

  • Bitcoin (BTC)

  • Avalanche (AVAX)

  • ChainLink (LINK)

  • Shiba Inu (SHIB)

  • Conclusion

The cryptocurrency market has evolved into a gold mine for investors, with the exciting start of 2025 sparking renewed interest in various digital currencies. As investors carefully manage their portfolios, they are increasingly turning their attention to cryptocurrencies, which offer a wealth of opportunities beyond just the widely publicized Bitcoin. 

 

While Bitcoin continues to dominate headlines, many altcoins are also establishing robust foundations and potential for growth. Let’s see the latest discussions and trends shaping the current landscape of crypto space.

BlockDAG (BDAG)

BlockDAG is a modern network different from traditional blockchain technologies. It is a combination of modern decentralized ledger technologies that make it stand out from every other crypto project. This modern decentralized ledger is divided into two different camps, the UTXO model and the account-based model. 

 

In today’s decentralized ledgers, crypto projects use either UTXO or an account-based model, however, the BlockDAG network supports both at the same time. The UTXO in BlockDAG technology supports prompt and scalable payments. On the other hand, the EVM-compatible account-based system gives impressive support to standardized smart contracts. 

 

Currently, BlockDAG is going through an impressive presale phase and has raised over $177M. BDAG is in its crypto presale 2025 Batch 27—Phase 13 and priced at 0.0248 USDT. Furthermore, BlockDAG is more focused on the sustainability of the project and using the miner community. 75B coins are specifically allocated as mining rewards to encourage a robust network of miners. All these facts and features make the BlockDAG a number-one choice for potential investors looking for higher returns in 2025.

Ethereum (ETH)

Ethereum is currently demonstrating signs of recovery, trading around the $3,400 mark, even after experiencing a notable 12% decline in the aftermath of the Federal Reserve's recent announcements. The selling pressure has been predominantly driven by US investors, who have pulled out an astonishing $60.5 million from exchange-traded funds (ETFs). 

 

However, on the flip side, the data from exchange reserves suggests a robust strategy for dip-buying, indicating that not all investors are wary. Analysts are cautioning about the looming risk of a significant drop to $2,000 if the critical support level at $3,014 fails to hold. 

 

Despite these warnings, there remains a sense of optimism among investors, many of whom are eager to take advantage of potential buying opportunities during the dip.

Ripple (XRP)

Edo Farina, the CEO of Alpha Lions Academy, strongly encourages investors to acquire and hold a minimum of 1,000 XRP, which is currently valued at around $2,300. He anticipates that significant profits will emerge as Ripple, the company behind XRP, continues to broaden its partnerships with central banks globally. 

 

Farina emphasizes the importance of safeguarding these assets by opting for cold wallets instead of keeping them on exchanges, which can be more vulnerable to security breaches. He also advocates for adopting a long-term investment strategy, reminding investors to stay committed through the inevitable ups and downs of the market.

Dogecoin (DOGE)

A prominent analyst has made bold predictions that Dogecoin could soar to an impressive $80 between February and May of 2025. This forecast is grounded in a thorough analysis of historical data and the recurrent four-year cycle patterns observed in cryptocurrency markets. 

 

At present, Dogecoin is trading at $0.33, and indicators suggest that it may be experiencing a local bottom. These technical signals hint at a potential bullish reversal, creating an exciting prospect for investors looking to capitalize on the cryptocurrency's future movements.

Bitcoin (BTC)

Bitcoin has experienced a significant drop in value, plummeting from its all-time high of $108,364 to $92,118, largely driven by concerns surrounding a potential government shutdown in the United States. Influential figures in the crypto world, such as former Bitmex CEO Arthur Hayes and the controversial entrepreneur Martin Shkreli, are voicing their predictions of even steeper declines in the cryptocurrency's price. 

 

Despite these forecasts, supporters of former President Trump are holding onto a sense of optimism. They believe that his possible return to power could ignite a remarkable resurgence in the cryptocurrency market, setting the stage for a historic recovery by the year 2025.

Avalanche (AVAX)

Despite a notable decline of 27%, bringing AVAX down to $38, many analysts are maintaining a positive outlook on the cryptocurrency's future. This optimism is largely fueled by the Avalanche Foundation's recent announcement of a substantial $250 million investment dedicated to upgrading the blockchain.

 

The upgrade aims to enhance cost-efficiency and significantly boost scalability. As a result, experts are forecasting that AVAX could rebound to approximately $61.88 by December 23, with even more ambitious predictions suggesting it may reach $100 by the year 2026.

ChainLink (LINK)

Many analysts are optimistic about Chainlink (LINK) despite recent price changes. They expect the cryptocurrency to bounce back to around $35 by January 2025, with the possibility of reaching $75 by mid-2025. This positive outlook is based on Chainlink's strong fundamentals and the activity of large investors, known as "whales." There is also solid support at around $23, which adds to the belief in Chainlink's long-term growth potential.

Shiba Inu (SHIB)

Achieving early retirement with a target of $2 million is a goal that requires substantial returns for Shiba Inu investors. For instance, an initial investment of $10,000 at a certain price point, labeled as $0.000022, would necessitate the price of SHIB to rise to approximately $0.0044.

 

If investors decide to invest a larger amount, such as $50,000 or $100,000, the price of SHIB would need to experience even more dramatic increases, reaching levels of $0.00088 and $0.00044, respectively. These figures highlight the ambitious nature of such investments and the required price appreciation in order to meet retirement aspirations.

Conclusion

As we near 2025, the cryptocurrency market offers many options for both cautious and adventurous investors. Well-known cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin and Ethereum remain strong choices, with solid histories and wide acceptance. 

 

At the same time, newer options like Avalanche and Shiba Inu attract attention for their unique ideas and potential for growth. Each cryptocurrency comes with its own risks and rewards, so it’s important for investors to do thorough research before even deciding on passive income from crypto.

 

Understanding the technology behind each coin, market trends, and the basics of each project can improve your investment strategy. By considering your own risk tolerance and matching it with your investment choices, you can better manage the market's ups and downs, helping you to aim for long-term gains in this exciting and changing field.
