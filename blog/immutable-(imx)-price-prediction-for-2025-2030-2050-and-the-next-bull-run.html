If you are curious about IMX or are thinking about investing in this crypto, keep reading this blog. We will give an overview of Immutable X’s technology, analyze its price history, and discuss the IMX price predictions for 2025, 2030, 2050, and the next bull run. 

An Overview of Immutable X

Immutable X was launched in 2021 on the Ethereum blockchain. It's a Layer 2 solution that aims to enhance NFT transactions without any gas fees. The network offers high-speed transactions and leverages a zero-knowledge-proof system for security. 

 

IMX has the ability to process 9000 transactions per second while ensuring minimal environmental impact. IMX’s unparalleled performance and focus on sustainability have led to a surge in popularity especially in the Blockchain gaming industry. The following features further enhance the appeal of IMX:

 

  1. Users don’t have to incur high costs to trade or store NFTs
  2. IMX supports different types of digital assets like NFTs on the ERC-721 protocol and ERC-20 tokens
  3. IMX has strategic partnerships with Gamestop and Tencent
  4. Plans on introducing Immutable Passport and SDK

FAQs About Immutable (IMX) Crypto

1. What is unique about IMX?


IMX processes 9000 transactions per second at zero gas fees because of its zero-knowledge-proof technology.

 

2. Does IMX support cross-chain transactions?


Built on the Ethereum network, IMX focuses on NFTs on this network. Cross-chain compatibility has yet to be a feature.
