The cryptocurrency world is continuously evolving with high-end technology, and with each advancement, paying attention to the basics is necessary. One such basic and crucial factor is the transaction’s speed when dealing with cryptocurrencies. Ethereum network has to deal with this vulnerability along with high gas pricing; however, it has many other advantages over other platforms.  

 

When it comes to addressing the issue of slow transaction speed in the cryptocurrency world, a Layer-2 scaling network like Optimism emerges as the most promising solution. Optimism (OP) is the best option to invest in crypto projects when it comes to high-speed transactions, low gas fees, and high profitability. In this blog post, we will see the potential of the token and the Optimism (OP) price prediction for 2024, 2025, and 2030. 

Overview of Optimism (OP)

Optimism is an extension of the Ethereum network that allows developers and users to experience fast transaction speeds, high security, and low gas fee charges. The Optimism (OP) token is utilized for governance and can be traded on several well-known crypto exchanges, including Binance, Coinbase, Bybit, OKX, and many others. 

 

Optimism is a Layer-2 scaling network that was built by Ethereum developers for fast and scalable transactions. Optimism's EVM-equivalent structure predictably expands the capacity of your Ethereum applications. Dedicated to constructing a feasible environment through backward-looking public goods financing, Optimism is expanding Ethereum's current state to allocate resources for its future.

 

The best thing about Optimism is that it allows you to experience everything you can in Ethereum but with 10 times lower rates. Similar to the Ethereum network, Optimism’s users can deal with the DeFi Apps and can trade, mint, and collect NFTs, etc.

At the time of the lunch in May 2022, the initial value of the token was $4.57, but within hours, it dropped to $0.79 before bouncing back to around $1.37 by June 3.

How To Use Optimism?

Optimism is designed to provide a seamless experience for users, as it closely mirrors the Ethereum mainnet. When using Optimism, your address remains the same as your Ethereum mainnet address. Additionally, Optimism's blockchain explorer closely resembles Etherscan, which is the blockchain explorer for the Ethereum mainnet. This similarity helps users navigate and monitor activities on Optimism's network with familiarity and ease. Optimism is compatible with various decentralized finance wallets, with MetaMask being the most widely used option among them.

 

To configure your wallet for Optimism, you can manually adjust the settings. However, a simpler method is available. Visit a DeFi application such as SushiSwap (SUSHI) and specify that you want to use Optimism as your network. By selecting this option on the website, your MetaMask will automatically add the network and switch to it, making the process seamless and hassle-free.

To start using Optimism, you will need to have ether (ETH) to cover gas fees, just like how it works on the Ethereum mainnet. To accomplish this, you have the option to move ETH to your MetaMask from a different blockchain, like Ethereum mainnet, or from alternative layer 2 platforms such as Avalanche. To move funds from another chain, you can utilize any of the bridges that offer support for Optimism.

Conclusion

Optimism (OP) stands out as a promising Layer-2 scaling network that addresses Ethereum's challenges of slow transaction speeds and high gas fees. Its ability to enhance transaction efficiency while maintaining security and reducing costs makes it an attractive option for both developers and investors. As the cryptocurrency market continues to evolve, the price predictions for Optimism (OP) reflect a positive outlook, with potential growth in the coming years. Whether you're a developer looking to leverage its capabilities or an investor seeking profitable opportunities, Optimism (OP) offers a compelling proposition in the fast-paced world of cryptocurrency.
