Best Penny Cryptos to Invest in Before 2026 for High Rewards

Release Date: September 3, 2025

  • What is a Penny Crypto?

  • 1. BlockDAG (BDAG): A Future Network

  • 2. Shiba Inu (SHIB): Meme with Momentum

  • 3. Kaspa (KAS): Speed Meets Security

  • 4. Bitgert (BRISE): Zero-Cost Blockchain

  • 5. VeChain (VET)

  • 6. AllianceBlock Nexera (NXRA)

  • 7. Akash Network (AKT)

  • Which Penny Crypto has 1000x Potential?

As of 2025, the cryptocurrency space remains in its growth stage, with new and seasoned investors seeking to find the next hidden gem. One of the brightest spots in this space is penny cryptocurrencies tokens that are less than $1 and possess amazing growth potential. With thousands of options, though, it may be difficult to know which ones are worth your time.

 

So, which best penny crypto has 1000x potential? Which is the best penny crypto to buy now? In this article, we’ll break down the most promising projects that could explode in value over the coming years. We’ll also highlight why BlockDAG has quickly emerged as a strong contender alongside other penny coins ready to make waves in 2025.

What is a Penny Crypto?

A penny crypto is one that trades for less than one dollar. These tend to be newer or lower market-cap, and therefore, they carry a higher risk but with the higher potential. Most of those who had gotten in early on something like Dogecoin or Shiba Inu made enormous amounts of money simply for being early before the mainstream caught on.

 

Penny cryptocurrencies are attractive for a multiple of reasons:

 

  • Low barrier to entry: You can create thousands of tokens for a small investment.
  • Upside potential: Small price increases can equal big percentage gains.
  • Access to innovation: The majority of these projects are working on bleeding edge tech or expanding into untapped niches.

 

But all penny coins aren't created equal. To find the best penny crypto to invest in, you need to look at fundamentals, utility, team quality, and market momentum.

1. BlockDAG (BDAG): A Future Network

BlockDAG (BDAG) is gaining momentum as one of the best penny coins of 2025. Unlike the traditional blockchains, BlockDAG employs a Directed Acyclic Graph structure to facilitate faster, more scalable, and more energy efficient transactions.

 

If you’re asking which penny crypto has 1000x potential, BlockDAG has many advantages: fresh tech, solid roadmap, and increasing investor interest. While no investment is guaranteed, BlockDAG is certainly one of the more exciting picks this year.

2. Shiba Inu (SHIB): Meme with Momentum

Shiba Inu started as a meme coin but has evolved into a real ecosystem. For 2025, it's still among the best penny crypto assets for those who believe in the possibility of community and narrative.

 

With the launch of its own Layer 2 network, Shibarium, and continued expansion in decentralized services like ShibaSwap, SHIB now offers utility in real life. Being cheap and highly liquid, it is a penny cryptocurrency worth buying now for investors who want to gain exposure to one of the strongest crypto communities.

 

Even though not as overlooked as newer projects, Shiba Inu is otherwise a solid play for widespread adoption and continued expansion.

3. Kaspa (KAS): Speed Meets Security

Kaspa is a DAG based cryptocurrency more likely to BlockDAG in theory. It is focused around instant time confirmation. Kaspa has a strong use case as a high speed and secure layer for financial applications, gaming, and beyond. Its GHOSTDAG protocol allows for several blocks to be present simultaneously, which causes drastic latency reduction.

 

At a cost significantly below $1, Kaspa is continuing to appeal to miners and developers, adding to its long term credibility. While it may not get the same marketing push as some of the other coins, it has good fundamentals that earn it a place among the best penny crypto to invest in 2025.

4. Bitgert (BRISE): Zero-Cost Blockchain

Bitgert is another newcomer to the penny crypto spotlight. The project boasts a zero cost blockchain with more than 100,000 transactions per second. If accurate, this would be faster than most other networks in space.

 

The people behind Bitgert are also working on a complete DeFi ecosystem, including wallets, staking utilities, and even a decentralized exchange. With such a grand vision, BRISE is a buy now penny crypto for those who would like to invest in a full stack platform.

 

Its very low cost per token gives it enormous room for appreciation, especially if its ecosystem explodes. While riskier, the potential reward is high.

5. VeChain (VET)

VeChain is a penny crypto already being used in the real world. With the main emphasis on supply chain management and verification networks, VeChain has big name corporate partners like Walmart China and BMW.

 

Unlike speculative meme tokens, VeChain brings real  value, which adds a level of stability to its investment appeal. It may not provide the 1000x gains as newer tokens can, but it’s among the best penny crypto to invest in for those who want lower risk with decent growth.

 

If adoption continues to expand in Asia and beyond, VET could become a global standard in logistics blockchain solutions.

6. AllianceBlock Nexera (NXRA)

AllianceBlock Nexera token is where traditional finance and decentralized platforms meet. It's a compliance based platform that wants to tokenize real  assets like stocks, real estate, and more.

 

NXRA is a penny crypto to invest in today because of its focus on compliance, something that most DeFi projects lack. As the global financial institutions begin to look more and more at tokenized assets, Nexera could be set for some significant growth.

 

This token is an excellent long term wager with increasingly mainstream investors entering the crypto space looking for DeFi compliant platforms.

7. Akash Network (AKT)

Decentralized computing is one of the hottest topics in 2025, and Akash Network is at the forefront. It offers a decentralized alternative to cloud providers like AWS or Google Cloud by allowing developers to rent out computing power from others.

 

The growing demand for Web3 infrastructure could just make Akash the next penny cryptocurrency to blow up. With more dApps, metaverse experiences, and DeFi protocols deploying, demand for responsive compute power is growing fast.

 

AKT remains below $1 and is one of the best penny cryptos with potential for a great long term future, especially in the decentralized infrastructure sector.

Which Penny Crypto has 1000x Potential?

The search for the best penny crypto in 2025 isn't merely a low price, it's about seeing tech, community, and real world use cases. Coins like BlockDAG are forging with 1000x potential and new architecture, whereas initiatives like Kaspa and Bitgert are building faster, more scalable networks.

 

Others like VeChain, NXRA, and Akash are focusing on utility, delivering solutions that bridge blockchain to mainstream business needs.

 

If you're wondering which penny crypto has 1000x potential, BlockDAG stands out due to its state of the art tech, early stage valuation, and rapidly growing support. But each of these best crypto projects here has its own advantages and even one of them can be the next penny cryptocurrency to explode.
