What Is Mining? And How To Make Money Mining Cryptos?

Crypto mining is the process of validating crypto transactions on a blockchain network and then adding them to a public ledger. Crypto miners have to deploy machines that solve complex mathematical problems in the form of crypto hashes, in return, these miners are rewarded with new coins which are released into circulation.

 

Mining cryptos is a very profitable way to make money. However, profitability relies on several factors like hardware efficiency, choosing the right cryptocurrency, electric costs, and market conditions. This is where BlockDAG shines in the market.

One standout feature of BlockDAG is its emphasis on efficiency and speedy transactions which has made BDAG an easily mined cryptocurrency. Unlike many cryptocurrencies, BDAG is not only easy to mine but is also highly profitable.  

 

Your profitability also depends on your mining hardware and BlockDAG’s ASIC miners are perfect because of their low power consumption and high hash rates. You can choose from the following range of miners to find the one that perfectly suits your needs:

 

BlockDAG’s X10 Miner:

It's the perfect small crypto mining device for anyone new to crypto mining. X10 Miner is compact but powerful and allows you to mine up to 200 BDAG coins every day. X10 is easy to set up via Bluetooth or Wi-Fi and ensures smooth integration with BlockDAG’s X1 Miner app. It is perfect for beginners as it offers a low-cost but scalable entry point into crypto mining. 

 

BlockDAG’s X30 Miner:

If you want to take your mining beyond, you can choose BlockDAG’s X30 Miner. It increases a miner’s earnings by allowing them to mine up to 600 BDAG coins daily! Its advanced ASICS technology helps to maximize efficiency and security within the BlockDAG ecosystem. This option is ideal for intermediates and seasoned miners. 

 

BlockDAG’s X100 Miner: 

Are you a seasoned miner or want to mine on a larger scale? BlockDAG’s X100 ASIC Miner is the perfect solution for you. It delivers unprecedented performance and allows you to mine up to 2000 BDAG coins per day. The X100 Miner also leverages ASIC technology to handle large volumes of transactions while maintaining energy efficiency. Its innovative technology is ideal for long-term mining needs. 

 

If you are skeptical about investing in hardware, BlockDAG also offers a cloud-based solution. The X1 Miner app allows you to mine 20 BDAG coins daily directly from your phone. Available on iOS and Android, the X1 Miner App is an accessible and sustainable path for everyone. 

How To Start Crypto Mining?

Once you have chosen the right crypto miner whether it's the X10, X30, or X100; you can follow the instructions below to set up your ASIC miner and start earning BDAG coins efficiently. 

 

Step 1: Set up your rig

 

Firstly, you need to connect your miner to a reliable power source because stable power will ensure uninterrupted mining operations. You should then connect your miner to the internet using an ethernet cable and assign static IPs to your miners. Your setup will be complete once you use the default credentials provided in the miner’s manual to log in. 

 

Step 2: Configure Your Mining Pool

 

After logging in, you need to configure the mining pool by providing details like address, username, wallet details, etc. Once you configure your mining pool,  you will be able to start earning BDAG coins!

 

Step 3: Start Mining & Monitor performance:

 

Once set up, you can start your mining operations, and the earnings from your activities will be sent directly to your wallet. You can also set up a computer as a monitoring tool to ensure optimal performance.
