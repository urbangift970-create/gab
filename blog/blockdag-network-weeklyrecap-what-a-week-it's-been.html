BlockDAG Network #WeeklyRecap: What a Week It’s Been!

Release Date: August 16, 2024

  • Monday: Wrapping Up the $2 Million Giveaway

  • Tuesday: A Deep Dive into the Crypto World

  • Wednesday: The Presale Is Heating Up!

  • Thursday: Celebrating Our Winners — It’s All About YOU!

  • Looking Ahead: The Future Is Bright!

  • Keep Believing, Keep Supporting

Hey BlockDAG Network family! It’s Friday, and you know what that means — it’s time for our #WeeklyRecap! This week has been nothing short of incredible, and we’re thrilled to share all the exciting developments with you. Whether you’ve been with us from the start or just joined the #BlockDAGArmy, we’re grateful for your support, and we can’t wait to see where this journey takes us next.



 

Monday: Wrapping Up the $2 Million Giveaway

We kicked off the week with a bang by announcing the conclusion of our massive $2 million giveaway competition. This six-month journey started as a bold move, a leap of faith when BlockDAG was just getting off the ground. Fast forward to today, and we’re blown away by what we’ve accomplished together — over 100,000 members in the #BlockDAGArmy, more than $66.5 million raised, and a global reach that’s only getting bigger. The giveaway has officially ended, and 50 lucky winners will soon be receiving their life-changing $40,000 prizes. It’s a game-changer for them, and we’re beyond excited to celebrate this milestone with you.

 

Reflecting on where we started, it’s clear that the decision to launch this giveaway wasn’t just about marketing — it was about believing in our community and in the potential of what we could build together. Every participant in this competition played a crucial role in driving BlockDAG to where it is today. We took a leap of faith, and it’s paying off in ways we could have only dreamed of back in February.

Tuesday: A Deep Dive into the Crypto World

On Tuesday, we took a step back from the usual BlockDAG updates to explore the broader cryptocurrency and blockchain industry. We didn’t shy away from discussing the current state of the market, acknowledging the uncertainty that’s got Bitcoin struggling to reclaim its position above $60k. But here’s the silver lining — this is the perfect time to invest in a presale project like BlockDAG. Think of it as planting seeds in fertile ground; when the market turns bullish again, those who got in early will reap the rewards. We made the case for BlockDAG as a long-term store of value, a smart play in the unpredictable world of crypto.

 

Our team knows that the crypto market is often like a stormy sea — unpredictable, turbulent, but also full of opportunity for those who know how to navigate it. We’re steering BlockDAG through these waters with a clear vision and strategy. And right now, while the waves are choppy, is the ideal time to jump on board for those looking for long-term growth. We’re not just another project; we’re a lifeboat in the storm, ready to sail into calmer, more prosperous waters.



 

Wednesday: The Presale Is Heating Up!

Wednesday brought another important update — our presale is selling out FAST. We know how important it is to get the most bang for your buck, and that’s why we emphasized the urgency of buying or boosting your BDAG holdings before the price jumps. With only 25 million coins left in Batch 21,time is running out to secure your BDAG at the current low price. Every step we take, we get closer to our goals, and every coin you buy now will be worth more when we reach the next batch.

 

The excitement within our community is palpable. We’re watching the clock, counting down to the next price increase, and we want everyone to have the chance to maximize their investment before it happens. It’s like a race to the top, and we’re all climbing together — each coin you buy today is a step closer to reaching the summit of your financial goals.

Thursday: Celebrating Our Winners — It’s All About YOU!

Thursday was a day of celebration as we announced the 50 lucky winners of our $2 million giveaway. Each winner received a personal email letting them know they’d won $40,000 in BDAG coins. It was an emotional moment for us, as we reflected on how far we’ve come together. This week’s #InspiringInvestment Story was dedicated to you, our supporters. You believed in us from the beginning, and your belief is what’s driving BlockDAG Network forward.

 

We shared the story of how investing as little as $100 in BDAG could turn into a $40,000 windfall — proof that smart, strategic choices can lead to extraordinary results. But even if you weren’t among the 50 winners, you’re still a winner in our eyes. Everyone who has bought BDAG during our presale is poised for greatness. As we move closer to launch, we’re confident that your initial investment will multiply, much like the early adopters of Bitcoin and Ethereum have inspired us all to keep pushing forward.

 

Our message is clear: at BlockDAG, everyone’s a winner. Whether you’ve secured a $40,000 prize or are watching your BDAG holdings grow, your faith in us is what fuels our success. This is more than just a transaction — it’s a partnership, and we’re thrilled to be on this journey with you.

Looking Ahead: The Future Is Bright!

We’re wrapping up this week with a sense of excitement for what’s to come. BlockDAG has so much more in store, and we’re eager to share it with you. In just a week, we’ll be launching a surprise product that wasn’t even on our roadmap — yes, you read that right! This launch is going to be both fun and groundbreaking, and you’ll want to be among the first to experience it.

 

We’re also preparing to release a new team video that gives you an inside look at the incredible people behind BlockDAG, including our CEO, Antony Turner. Transparency, community, and trust are at the heart of everything we do, and we want you to feel as connected to our team as we do to you.

 

But that’s not all — our journey is far from over. We have big plans, exciting developments, and more milestones to achieve together. Whether it’s through innovative product launches, expanding our community, or driving forward in the crypto space, BlockDAG is committed to creating value and delivering on our promises. And as we continue to grow, we want you to know that your role in this journey is more important than ever.

Keep Believing, Keep Supporting

As we close out this week, we want to thank you for being a part of the BlockDAG journey. Your support means the world to us, and together, we’re building something truly special. If you haven’t already, now is the perfect time to buy more BDAG at the current price — before the next batch starts and the price goes up. The long-hold game is where true wealth is built, and we’re confident that BlockDAG Network is the smart move you’ve been looking for.

 

Thank you for following our #WeeklyRecap. We’ll be back with more exciting news and stories soon. Keep following, keep sharing, and keep believing — because here at BlockDAG, we’re certified #1!
