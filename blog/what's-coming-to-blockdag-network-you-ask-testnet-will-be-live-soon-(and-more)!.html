What’s Coming To BlockDAG Network You Ask? Testnet Will Be Live Soon (and more)!

Release Date: September 12, 2024

  • What Can You Do on BlockDAG Explorer?

  • Devnet to Testnet: The Countdown Begins

  • Dev Releases: What’s New in the World of BlockDAG?

  • $72 Million Raised and Counting — The BlockDAG Presale Success!

  • The Future Is Now — And You’re at the Center of It

  • We’re Just Getting Started — See You Soon!

Hey BlockDAG readers! Welcome to another exciting update from your favorite decentralized network, BlockDAG Network. We’re thrilled to announce some major developments that are just around the corner, and we want *you* to be the first to know.

 

Today, we’re diving into the game-changing milestone we’re about to hit — our imminent Testnet launch! Yes, you heard that right — on September 20th, we’re making the leap from devnet to testnet. But that’s not all. We’re giving you the inside scoop on all the cool dev releases, our incredible $72 million presale milestone (approaching), and the exciting future we’re building together.



 

What’s the Big Deal About Testnet?

Okay, let’s break it down. If you’re wondering what all this testnet hype is about, think of devnet as the sandbox where our developers have been building the nuts and bolts of BlockDAG. It’s like a rehearsal before the big show. We’ve been playing around, testing, tweaking, and making sure everything is running like a well-oiled machine. But now? Now we’re ready to take it to the next level.

 

Testnet is where things get real. It’s the “almost there” stage — a fully functioning environment where we invite you to come in and try things out.It’s where developers, testers, and curious crypto heads get to poke around, simulate transactions, run smart contracts, and really see what BlockDAG Network can do.

 

And after testnet? The mainnet, where we go LIVE, and the magic starts for real. But right now, September 20th is the day to mark. Because when the BlockDAG Testnet goes live, so does BlockDAG Explorer, giving you full access to all the core features.

What Can You Do on BlockDAG Explorer?

If you’ve been itching to see what BlockDAG is all about, the BlockDAG Explorer will be your window into our decentralized world. Here’s what you can expect:

 

- Block Details Viewing: Dive deep into every single block in the BlockDAG Blockchain, with detailed information and full transparency. Get the full breakdown of what’s happening on every level.

 

- Transaction Tracking: Want to track transactions in real-time? You’ll be able to view and monitor both UTXO and account-based transactions on the fly, with full support for the Ethereum Virtual Machine (EVM).

 

- Faucet Functionality: Need to test some transactions? Use the faucet to transfer test tokens within the testnet environment. It’s your chance to experiment, risk-free.

 

- EVM Support: If you’re into smart contracts, you’re in luck. Our EVM support lets you simulate and test smart contracts and EVM-based applications within the BlockDAG ecosystem. Let your creativity run wild!

 

- Real-Time Monitoring: Stay in the loop with real-time transaction updates. Whether you’re testing or just keeping an eye on things, you’ll always know what’s happening as it happens.

Devnet to Testnet: The Countdown Begins

So, what does this all mean? We’re just weeks away from our Testnet Era, and that’s huge. The devnet has been great for laying the groundwork, but testnet is when we really open the doors to the community. It’s your chance to get hands-on with BlockDAG Network before the mainnet launch.

 

And trust us — this is no ordinary testnet. The team has been working hard to ensure that every feature, every function, and every transaction runs smoothly. We’re fine-tuning the system to make sure that by the time we hit mainnet, BlockDAG is ready to take on the decentralized world full force.

Dev Releases: What’s New in the World of BlockDAG?

While we’re on the topic of progress, let’s talk about the latest dev releases. We’ve been constantly iterating and improving, and our dev team has been crushing it. Some highlights from our latest development cycle include:

 

- Enhanced EVM Support: We’re making it easier than ever to build and deploy EVM-based applications. Whether you’re a developer or just a fan of decentralized apps, BlockDAG will be a playground for innovation.

 

- Improved Scalability: We’ve optimized the BlockDAG network to handle even higher transaction volumes. Think of it as your supercharged freeway — no traffic jams, just smooth, scalable performance.

 

- Security Enhancements: Because security is everything. Our team has implemented advanced security protocols to ensure that the BlockDAG network remains resilient and protected against any threats.

 

- User Interface Upgrades: We’re all about making the experience seamless. The BlockDAG Explorer will come with a polished, intuitive interface that even a first-timer can navigate effortlessly.

 

We’re also gearing up for even more exciting releases and features as we inch closer to mainnet. There’s a lot happening behind the scenes, and we can’t wait to roll it all out for you.



 

$72 Million Raised and Counting — The BlockDAG Presale Success!

Let’s take a moment to celebrate a massive milestone — our presale is officially about to touch $72 million raised! And honestly? We’re just getting started. This overwhelming support from the community is not only a vote of confidence but a clear sign that BlockDAG is the future.

 

With the presale continuing to gain momentum, we’re pumped about what’s coming next. The funds are helping us drive the project forward, fuel innovation, and build a decentralized ecosystem that’s second to none.

The Future Is Now — And You’re at the Center of It

We’re not just building a blockchain. We’re building a movement. BlockDAG Network is here to redefine the future of decentralized finance, and we want you at the heart of it all. Whether you’re a developer, an investor, or just someone curious about the power of decentralization, BlockDAG is for**you**.

 

The Testnet Era is your chance to dive in, get hands-on, and see firsthand what makes BlockDAG different. You’ll be able to test, explore, and interact with a system that’s faster, more scalable, and more secure than anything you’ve seen before. And once we hit mainnet, it’s full steam ahead into the decentralized future.

We’re Just Getting Started — See You Soon!

We’re on the cusp of something incredible, and we couldn’t be more excited to share it with you. Mark your calendars for September 20th, because the BlockDAG Testnet is about to change everything. Stay tuned, stay connected, and get ready to experience the future.

 

We’ll be back with another update soon, so keep following, keep sharing, and keep believing. BlockDAG is certified #1, and we’re taking this movement to the next level — with YOU at the center.
