Top 11 New ICO Cryptos of 2024: Discover the Next Crypto Game-Changing Projects

Release Date: August 8, 2024

  • 1. BlockDAG (BDAG)

  • 2. Bitcoin Minetrix (BTCMTX)

  • 3. Dogeverse

  • 4. Borroe Finance (ROE)

  • 5. Algotech (ALGT)

  • 6. BlastUP

  • 7. DeeStream (DST)

  • 8. eTukTuk (TUK)

  • 9. KangaMoon (KANG)

  • 10. 5th Scape

  • 11. Bitbot (BITBOT)

  • Conclusion

 

With the bubbling market in 2024, traders and investors are back to find and benefit from the new ICO crypto in the market. Early investors can benefit from huge returns as they take the risk and position themselves as early adopters of the project. The primary purpose of the ICO (Initial Coin Offering) is to raise capital for the project while also providing opportunities for the investors to enjoy the benefits if the project sees early success in the market. 

 

However, thousands of ICOs are launched every month, and it is difficult to choose and invest in the right project at the right time. With many scams in the crypto market, it is necessary to research the team, planning, and strategy of the project before deciding to invest in any of them. In this blog post, we will see the top Crypto ICOs in 2024 that are reliable, authentic, and capable of providing potential returns on investment.  

 

  1. BlockDAG (BDAG)
  2. Bitcoin Minetrix (BTCMTX)
  3. DOGEVERSE
  4. Borroe Finance (ROE)
  5. Algotech (ALGT)
  6. BlastUP
  7. DeeStream (DST)
  8. eTukTuk (TUK)
  9. KangaMoon (KANG)
  10. 5th Scape
  11. Bitbot (BITBOT)

1. BlockDAG (BDAG)

BlockDAG is in its initial phase before making the coin available to the public. The success of the BDAG crypto ICO has caught the attention of many potential investors and traders. Their main objective is not only to raise the initial funds but also to create a robust community of supporters to make the project more sustainable. The presale allocation of the BlockDAG coin is 33% of the maximum token supply, which is set aside for marketing purposes. The planned and executed marketing strategy is aimed at expanding and strengthening the project over various social media platforms before its launch. The best part of BlockDAG is the diverse options for the community, which include the following;

 

  • Coin Investment 
  • Mining for beginners and experts 
  • Dedicated miner units 

 

BlockDAG has entered the 10th batch of its presale with a total worth of coin sales of over $23 million. The strategic approach of the BDAB coin takes this project ahead of its competitors, such as by planning to implement the coin vesting period for a sustainable environment. Where all other new coins can’t bear the uncertainty of the market, such strategies help the project to tackle it with resilience and robustness. 

2. Bitcoin Minetrix (BTCMTX)

Bitcoin Minetrix has already raised over $13 million in its Initial Coin Offering. The token's price remained fixed at $0.0148 until its conclusion. The project used Ethereum smart contracts to facilitate and simplify the entry of crypto miners. However, even though it is user-friendly, the presale of BTCMTX has marked a crucial point in meeting the reality of the mining landscape in the competitive market. The recent Bitcoin halving event in April 2024 has impacted most of the cryptocurrencies in the market, including a significant reduction in BTC mining rates from 6.25 to 3.125 per block. 

3. Dogeverse

There is a history of Doge-based crypto coins that significantly reward early investors with lucrative returns on investment. However, these meme coins always face limitations because of their fundamental technology. Dogeverse is yet another meme coin that will be available to traders and investors on the world's leading 6 blockchain networks. This multi-chain ability of this coin connects the meme coin community through various blockchains, while its magnificent utility makes it stand out from the potential competitors in the market. The Dogeverse has a limited and fixed 15% out of the total supply for presale. However, the remaining coins will be used for the following purposes;

 

  • Strategic Marketing
  • Development of the project
  • Liquidity
  • Staking and exchange listings 

 

This coin, with its typical meme coin nature, is believed to benefit early investors. Traders and investors can buy a presale Dogverse coin at MATIC, BNB, or ETH. 

4. Borroe Finance (ROE)

Borrow Finance is a comprehensive marketplace based on Artificial Intelligence funding for Web3 users so that they can sell their future earnings. The feature that makes ROE unique is the tokenization of future invoices into NFTs, which can be quickly sold out within a peer-to-peer marketplace. The ROE simplifies this process by effectively combining the blockchain and AI into a system. As per experts, this model will overcome the limitations of the traditional model of finance in Web3. For a safe and secure transactional environment, AI is used for risk evaluation, while blockchain is used for enhanced security. With 25% bonus offerings using a promo code, ROE is currently in its 5th stage with an operating price of $0.02. It is expected to rise above $0.025 at its launch. 

5. Algotech (ALGT)

Algotech is a rising star in the crypto market, and with its presales of over $4.2 million, it has attracted whales in the market. The term “Whales” in the crypto world refers to the big investors holding a significant portion of digital assets. Their engagement in the presale of the project makes it more authentic and appealing to the market. The involvement of whales in the presales of ALGT is considered as a turning point painting a sustainable future of the project. The democratizing of algorithm trading advanced tools, along with other essential factors, Algotech indicates the unexpected positive growth of the project in the near future. With the continuous evolution of the blockchain space, it would be interesting to reap the benefits in the upcoming months.

6. BlastUP

BlastUP was specifically designed to support Blast's ecosystem launchpad platform and provide a structured environment for new projects aimed at raising capital and community involvement at the same time. BlastUP is currently in the 8th stage of the presale, operating at the price of $0.07. The project will allocate tokens for sale to token holders. By staking BlastUP tokens, you can earn loyalty rewards and interest. This means that if you invest in BlastUP tokens and hold them for a certain period, you will get additional tokens as a reward. Additionally, you can earn interest on your tokens by staking them, which is a great way to grow your investment over time. The utility of the BlastUP has many aspects, including,

 

  • Rewards
  • Buybacks 
  • Access to IDOs
  • Community incentives 
  • Passive income 

7. DeeStream (DST)

Investors and traders can benefit from the presale of DST by investing in the project. They can participate in a swap service and rewards program and share in the generated fees. With a reasonable ICO price of $0.035, DeeStream presents an attractive investment opportunity for those who want to align their success with the platform's long-term sustainability and prosperity. It stands at the front line of the Web3 innovation, stimulating the traditional models of streaming and indicating concerns about price rationality. DST opens doors to opportunity for investors through decentralized governance and extra rewards by transforming the landscape of streaming for users. 

8. eTukTuk (TUK)

eTukTuk is an exceptional eco-friendly crypto project in the market. It has caught the audience's attention by solving real-life issues such as global warming and climate change. It plans to introduce electric Tuk Tuk vehicles with special charging stations across the country. TUK is a native token of the project that aims to be used as a means of exchange within the ecosystem. In order to take part in the presale of TUK, you need to visit their presale official website. You can buy the token by connecting your Ethereum-based wallet. The TUK token has already achieved more than the $3.3 million mark throughout its presale. 21.5% of the total supply is directly allocated for staking rewards, 7% for liquidity, and 9.75% for presale, highlighting a balanced distribution approach for a sustainable future. 

9. KangaMoon (KANG)

KANG is a rising meme coin in the crypto market that is a perfect blend of social interaction and play-to-earn games. By combining P2E gaming and social interaction, KANG is focused on engaging the community to create sustainable and long-term opportunities for investors. The early investors enjoyed a 50% ROI in the project's initial stage. Currently, KANGA is in its 5th stage of presale and has managed to raise more than $6.2 million throughout the presale. With the unique mix of P2E and social interaction, KANG is considered one of the top cryptos among all current meme coins in the market.

10. 5th Scape

5th Scape is all set to reshape the metaverse concept with the new token's presale in the crypto market. The core of their existence is a significant twist among VR (Virtual Reality) and AR (Augmented Reality) gaming content with blockchain for gaming enthusiasts to elevate their overall experience. They are creating and using their own set of VR hardware, including VR games, chairs, and headsets. This token will be used within the ecosystem to provide token holders with magnificent features, in-game assets, and exclusive access to various other characteristics. 5th Scape is currently in stage 6 of the presale, available at the price of $0.00376. The Hardcap for this project is $1.25 million. They already have marked over $5 million and aim for an overall target of $15 million throughout their presale period. 

11. Bitbot (BITBOT)

BITBOT is a new utility coin essential for the Bitbot, a new Telegram non-custodial AI bot. The BITBOT coin's ICO is becoming popular among traders for good reasons. It has managed to raise over $3.5 million in its presale, with an ongoing stage 13 presale at $0.0189. As per experts, the non-custodial approach and ultra-modern AI trading bot have the potential for the coin’s 100x surge later in the year 2024. Since the crypto market is expecting the next bull run, advanced AI bots are capable of thriving with the upcoming trend. According to the research, the AI trading bot sector can grow at over 37% compounded annual growth up to 2029. The main objective of Bitbot is to create equal opportunities for retail traders through advanced AI, making it one of the best ICO crypto projects for traders in 2024. 

Conclusion

New ICO cryptos are launched every other week, with some staying in the market for the short term and others for the long term. There are no shortages of ICOs available in the crypto market; however, choosing the right and best ICO crypto in 2024 is a crucial decision for traders and investors. This is why it is necessary to conduct thorough research about the presale ICO crypto before investing in one of them. As per our suggestion, the 11 top ICO cryptos in 2024 are mentioned above, with BlockDAG at the top of them because of its phenomenal presale achievements in 2024.
