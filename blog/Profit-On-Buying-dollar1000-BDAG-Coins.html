What Is BlockDAG?

BlockDAG’s native cryptocurrency is called BDAG coin, it revolves around the concepts of innovation and technology. Taking inspiration from Kaspa and Bitcoin, BDAG aims to become the faster and the safest layer 1 blockchain technology in the crypto market. BlockDAG moves forward with one goal to be better than its ideals, the coin’s developers are set to make the network more secure, decentralised and scalable to meet the demands of DApps and decentralised finance. 

 

Kaspa has set the standard for the PoW mechanism but BlockDAG is geared to take it further beyond. Using strategic enhancements, rapid innovations and unprecedented speed, BlockDAG aims to adopt the best features of Kapsa and enhance it to fit its ecosystem. 

 

At its core, BlockDAG is created on the proof of work consensus mechanism. However,  BDAG took one step ahead and merged the DAG (Directed Acyclic Graph) structure to safely conduct transactions over its network. 

 

No doubt, the crypto ecosystem is evolving at lightning speed, setting industry records. BlockDAG doesn't just want to be a network, it wants to be a holistic ecosystem. BlockDAG intends to introduce educational materials inspired by the user-centric approach of Platforms, decentralised applications and more to make sure that even the layman can play a role in its development. Now you know what is BlockDAG but now look at why you should be investing in this cryptocurrency.

Why Invest In BlockDAG?

The first thing to consider about BlockDAG is the vision of the developers, which have set their eyes on reaching $600 million in 2024. Next comes the promise of launching its mainnet within six months, further adding to the coin’s ecosystem. 

 

BlockDAG follows an ideology, to succeed one must pay extra attention to its community. The commitment to provide for its people has built a strong following for the coin. Not only this, but the coin has also gained the attention of the mining community, due to its latest X-Series crypto miners.


What Are BlockDAG’s X-Series Miners?
 

Operating on the PoW consensus mechanism BDAG offers its mining rigs to earn daily. The X-Series crypto miners are a pinnacle of innovation which makes crypto mining eco-friendly and energy-efficient. These mining machines have been made to use low amounts of energy while giving highly efficient results. 

 

 

BlockDAG offers various models of crypto mining rigs starting from X1, X10, X30 and X100. Each machine comes with different mining capabilities allowing users to earn specific amounts of BDAG coin daily. The X1 is a mobile mining application that allows you to mine up to 20 BDAG daily while consuming the minimum battery of your mobile phone. Furthermore, the backend operations of this mobile application are light on the processor making it highly energy efficient. 

 

Next comes the machine miners starting from X10 which allows you to make 200 BDAG per day at a hash rate of 100 MH/s. Next comes the BlockDAG X30, which has a hash rate of 280 GH/s allowing you to make up to 600 BDAG daily. The largest mining machine offered by BlockDAG is the X100, allowing you to make 2,000 BDAG daily at a hash rate of 2TH/s.

To become eligible to participate one must purchase at least $100 worth of BDAG coins and complete as many tasks as possible to increase your chances.
