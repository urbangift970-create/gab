Top 5 Crypto ETFs to Watch in 2024

Release Date: August 22, 2024

  • 1. iShares Bitcoin Trust (IBIT)

  • 2. ProShares Bitcoin Strategy ETF (BITO)

  • 3. Roundhill Bitcoin Covered Call Strategy ETF (YBTC)

  • 4. Global X Blockchain ETF (BKCH)

  • 5. VanEck Ethereum Strategy ETF (EFUT)

  • Conclusion

 

As the cryptocurrency market continues to evolve, investors are increasingly looking to Crypto ETFs (Exchange-Traded Funds) as a convenient way to gain exposure to digital assets. These ETFs offer a unique blend of accessibility, diversification, and potential for significant returns. There are several Crypto ETFs that stand out as particularly promising. Here's a look at the top 5 Crypto ETFs you should consider.

1. iShares Bitcoin Trust (IBIT)

Backed by the financial giant BlackRock, the iShares Bitcoin Trust (IBIT) has quickly become well-known in the world of Crypto ETFs. With assets under management (AUM) surpassing $17.3 billion, IBIT has gathered significant attention from investors. This ETF is specifically designed to track the CME CF Bitcoin Reference Rate.

One of the standout features of IBIT is its low cost, particularly in its early stages. The sponsor fee is 0.25%, but for the first $5 billion in AUM, the fee is reduced to just 0.12% for the initial 12 months. Additionally, the ETF boasts ample liquidity, with a 30-day bid-ask spread of only 0.03%, making it an attractive option for investors seeking both stability and growth.

2. ProShares Bitcoin Strategy ETF (BITO)

The ProShares Bitcoin Strategy ETF (BITO) holds the distinction of being the first U.S.-listed ETF linked to Bitcoin. While BITO does not hold physical Bitcoin, it provides exposure to the cryptocurrency through Bitcoin futures traded on the Chicago Mercantile Exchange (CME). This approach allows investors to speculate on Bitcoin's price movements or manage risk via hedging strategies.

What sets BITO apart is its active management strategy. The fund continually buys and sells CME Bitcoin futures contracts, a process known as "rolling." This strategy generates gains that are distributed monthly as dividend income, making BITO an attractive option for income-focused investors. For example, a recent distribution was approximately $1.77 per share, underscoring its potential as a source of income in the crypto space.

3. Roundhill Bitcoin Covered Call Strategy ETF (YBTC)

The Roundhill Bitcoin Covered Call Strategy ETF (YBTC) offers a unique approach by combining Bitcoin exposure with a covered call strategy. This ETF is designed to generate monthly income while providing capped upside exposure to Bitcoin. YBTC achieves this by holding synthetic long exposure to BITO's price returns through a combination of options strategies.

This approach allows YBTC to generate a significant income stream, currently boasting a 24.3% distribution rate. For investors seeking both income and exposure to Bitcoin without the complexities of direct investment, YBTC presents a captivating option. The covered call strategy, while capping potential gains, delivers steady cash premiums, making it a standout choice for those interested in income generation within the crypto market.

4. Global X Blockchain ETF (BKCH)

While many Crypto ETFs focus on holding Bitcoin or its futures, the Global X Blockchain ETF (BKCH) takes a different approach by investing in the broader crypto industry. This ETF offers exposure to a diversified portfolio of 25 crypto-related stocks, including prominent names like Coinbase, Riot Platforms, and Marathon Digital Holdings.

 

BKCH is designed to capitalize on the growth of blockchain technology and the increasing institutional interest in Bitcoin. With a focus on companies involved in cryptocurrency mining, exchanges, and other blockchain-related activities, BKCH provides an indirect but potentially lucrative way to benefit from the broader crypto ecosystem. The ETF charges a 0.5% expense ratio, reflecting its focus on high-growth opportunities within the industry.

5. VanEck Ethereum Strategy ETF (EFUT)

As Ethereum continues to play a central role in the decentralized finance (DeFi) ecosystem, the VanEck Ethereum Strategy ETF (EFUT) offers a way to gain exposure to Ethereum's price movements through futures contracts. Although spot Ethereum ETFs are yet to launch, EFUT provides an alternative by holding a portfolio of Treasury bills leveraged as collateral for CME Ether futures contracts.

EFUT is structured as a C-corporation, which offers greater tax efficiency for investors. While the ETF is relatively small, with just $26 million in AUM, it provides a modest 2.7% 30-day SEC yield. For investors looking to capitalize on Ethereum's potential while awaiting the debut of spot ETFs, EFUT is a noteworthy option.

Conclusion

As the cryptocurrency market matures, Crypto ETFs are becoming an increasingly popular way to gain exposure to digital assets. Whether you're interested in Bitcoin, Ethereum, or the broader blockchain industry, these top Crypto ETFs offer diverse opportunities for growth and income in 2024. Each ETF has its unique strengths, making it suitable for different investment strategies. As always, investors should carefully consider their individual goals and risk tolerance before diving into the exciting world of Crypto ETFs.
