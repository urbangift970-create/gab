#WeeklyRecap Of Our Big BlockDAG Week! Here’s Everything You Need To Know!

Release Date: September 13, 2024

  • Thursday:

  • What You Can Do on BlockDAG Testnet

  • Why Testnet Matters and What Comes Next

  • Friday:

  • Monday to Wednesday, and Everyday:

Hey BlockDAG readers! We’re back with another exciting weekly update from the BlockDAG Network. And believe us when we say this: last week was nothing short of monumental for us.

 

We’ve got big news, huge milestones, and a whole lot of updates to share with you that have taken place this last week. Buckle up, because this #WeeklyRecap article is going to cover everything from Monday to Friday, from our $72 million presale milestone to the imminent launch of our Testnet on September 20th. If you’ve been following along, this is the update you’ve been waiting for. If you’re new here, well, welcome to the party — we’re just getting started!



 

The BlockDAG Journey So Far

Before we dive into what’s coming, let’s take a quick moment to reflect. Just a few months ago, we were deep in the trenches of devnet, building and testing our infrastructure, working tirelessly to ensure that BlockDAG would stand out as a true leader in decentralized finance. We’ve been laying the groundwork, and the excitement has been building around what’s to come. It’s been a journey, but every step has been worth it.

 

Fast forward to today, and we’ve officially hit a major milestone — $72 million raised in our presale. Let that sink in for a second. $72 millionmeans that people believe in BlockDAG. They see the potential, they see the future we’re building, and they want in. It’s not just another number, it’s validation that BlockDAG Network is a serious contender in the decentralized space.

Thursday:

We Announced that Testnet Season Is Coming — Mark Your Calendars for September 20th

 

The real excitement is about to begin. We’ve been talking about it, teasing it, and now it’s finally happening. On September 20th, we’ll be officially moving from devnet into testnet, and we couldn’t be more pumped!

 

If you’re wondering what all the testnet hype is about, let’s break it down. Think of devnet as the playground — this is where our developers have been building, testing, and making sure everything is working smoothly. It’s like rehearsing for a big performance, running through every line, every cue, and making sure that when it’s time for the spotlight, we’re ready.

 

But testnet? That’s the next level. This is where we open up the doors, and you — yes, YOU — get to interact with the system. You’ll be able to run transactions, explore the blockchain, and even test out your own smart contracts. It’s the closest thing to the real deal without going full-on mainnet. It’s like taking a new sports car for a spin before the big race — everything is primed and ready, but you get a sneak peek before it’s released to the masses.

What You Can Do on BlockDAG Testnet

So, what exactly can you expect when BlockDAG Testnet goes live on September 20th? Here’s a sneak peek at what’s coming your way:

 

- Block Details Viewing: You’ll be able to explore every single block in the BlockDAG Blockchain with full transparency. Think of it as being able to peek under the hood of every transaction happening on the network. The best part? You can see it all in real time.

 

- Transaction Tracking: Ever wanted to track transactions in real time? On testnet, you’ll be able to monitor both UTXO (Unspent Transaction Output) and account-based transactions live as they happen. Plus, with EVM support, we’re bringing you Ethereum-style functionality, meaning you can test and run your own smart contracts within the ecosystem.

 

- Faucet Functionality: Want to test out some transactions yourself? We’ve got you covered. Our faucet will let you transfer test tokens across the network, giving you a hands-on experience with how BlockDAG works. And since it’s testnet, there’s no risk — just fun experimentation.

 

- EVM Support for Smart Contracts: Speaking of smart contracts, our EVM (Ethereum Virtual Machine) support means you can simulate and test your decentralized applications directly on BlockDAG’s network. Whether you’re a developer or just curious about how smart contracts work, you’ll get to dive in and see the power of decentralized tech firsthand.

 

- Real-Time Monitoring: Keep track of everything as it happens. With real-time transaction updates, you’ll always be in the loop and able to see how the network is performing. It’s like having a backstage pass to the world’s most innovative decentralized system.



 

Why Testnet Matters and What Comes Next

Testnet isn’t just a technical milestone — it’s a massive leap toward our ultimate goal: mainnet. This is where we refine the system, stress-test it under real-world conditions, and ensure that everything is rock solid before we hit the main stage. We want BlockDAG to be flawless when we go live, and testnet is where we make sure that happens.

 

Once we’re live on testnet, you’ll have the opportunity to engage with BlockDAG in ways you’ve never seen before. From running transactions to testing decentralized applications (dApps), it’s all about bringing the power of BlockDAG Network into your hands. This is YOUR chance to be part of something truly groundbreaking.



 

Friday:

We Can Officially Say Hit $72 Million in Our Presale! What’s Next?

 

Now, let’s talk about the $72 million we’ve raised in our presale. That number is more than just a milestone — it’s a testament to the growing belief in BlockDAG’s vision. It’s proof that the world is waking up to the potential of decentralized finance, and BlockDAG is leading the charge.

 

Raising $72 million means we’re not just talking about the future — we’re building it. These funds are fueling our development, expanding our team, and allowing us to push the boundaries of what decentralized tech can do. From scalability to security, every dollar is helping us take BlockDAG to the next level.

 

And we’re not stopping here. There’s so much more in store as we continue to build out the BlockDAG ecosystem. Our presale success is just the beginning of something bigger, something bolder. We’re on the brink of revolutionizing decentralized finance, and with $72 million, we’ve got the resources to make it happen.

Monday to Wednesday, and Everyday:

We Like to Remind You of Why BlockDAG is Different — and Why You’re at the Center of It

 

By now, you’re probably wondering: “Why BlockDAG? What makes it different from other decentralized platforms?” The answer is simple — we’re solving the problems traditional blockchain couldn’t. BlockDAG’s unique architecture allows for limitless scalability, faster transaction speeds, and real-time processing. No more slow, congested networks. No more high transaction fees. We’re building a decentralized ecosystem that actually works for the modern world.

 

But here’s the thing: you’re at the center of it all. Whether you’re a developer, an investor, or just someone who believes in the power of decentralization, you’re the reason we’re doing this. BlockDAG is built for the people, powered by the people. And as we enter the Testnet Era, you’ll be able to see that firsthand.

Next Week is Going to Be Even Bigger

If you thought this week was big, get ready — next week is going to be even bigger. With the Testnet launch on September 20th, we’re kicking off a whole new chapter in the BlockDAG Network story. More features, more announcements, and more opportunities for YOU to get involved.

 

We’re just getting started, and we’re thrilled to have you along for the ride.Keep following, keep sharing, and most importantly, keep believing. BlockDAG is certified #1, and together, we’re building the decentralized future.



 

Stay tuned for more updates, and we’ll be back with another exciting article soon!
