Our Live AMA Event: Talking About the #BlockDAGPhenomenon, Giving You All News & Insights

Release Date: July 31, 2024

  • A Shoutout to the Dream Team

  • Missed the Event? We’ve Got You Covered!

  • Why BlockDAG is a Game-Changer

  • The #BlockDAGPhenomenon: From Anonymity to Stardom

  • The Future is Always Bright at BlockDAG

With our first live event we wanted to bring together our team and community for an open and honest discussion. Let’s dive into the key moments!

 

 

The BlockDAG Phenomenon: A Live Event to Remember!

Hey blog readers & BlockDAG enthusiasts! Yesterday was a big day for BlockDAG Network — we hosted our first-ever live AMA (ask me anything) event, and it exceeded all expectations! It was incredible connecting with our community, answering your burning questions about past, present, and future of BlockDAG, and sharing all insights and visions into the exciting journey we’ve been on. We were thrilled to introduce ourselves live on camera and dive deep into our groundbreaking project.

A Shoutout to the Dream Team

First off, massive thanks to our social media wizards Nat and San. These two are the masterminds behind setting up and organizing this fantastic AMA event on our official Telegram channel. Another huge shoutout goes to our CEO, Anthony Turner, and our Advisory Board and Executive Team. Their passion and dedication to showing appreciation for our global organization and community made this event truly special. But the biggest thanks of all? That goes to YOU — our amazing and supportive community. Without your strength and belief in our mission, we wouldn’t be here today. You are the wind beneath our wings, and together, we’re soaring to new heights in the blockchain and digital finance world!

Missed the Event? We’ve Got You Covered!

If you couldn’t join us live, don’t worry! We’ve got the full recording ready for you. Check out the link here to watch it at your convenience. And for those who like a quick recap, here’s the rundown:

 

The BlockDAG AMA Recap: Our First Live Event

 

- Welcome and Thanks: We kicked off by thanking everyone for their incredible support. The energy was electric, and we could feel the love from all corners of the globe.

 

- Meet the Team: From Anthony Turner’s journey from SwissOne Capital to leading BlockDAG, to our experts like Steven on policy and partnerships, and Maurice Herlihy, an ex-Harvard & MIT professor now teaching blockchain and cryptography at Brown University. It was like meeting The Avengers of blockchain!

 

- Community Love: We took a moment to appreciate our 92,000 unique coin holders from over 150 countries, that’s what we mean when we say that we have #GoneGlobal! Our team predicts we’ll surpass 1 million holders by the end of our presale, rocketing us into the top 50 coins by market cap. That’s absolutely massive!

 

BlockDAG: The Company and its Unique Value

 

Antony outlined what makes BlockDAG unique:

 

- Hybrid Model: Combines Bitcoin’s gold-standard security with DAG’s speed and EVM interoperability.

 

- Innovations: Efficient proof-of-work (PoW) and continuous mining reward reduction ensure stability and a stable token price. Plus, we’re sharing fees with Layer 2 apps for better management.

 

- Company and Location: We operate as Dag Systems Limited, based in Seychelles, with a foundation in Switzerland. After our launch, we’ll function as a decentralized ecosystem.

 

- Launch in 2024: Yes, we’re on track to go live next year! Our presale is booming and raised over $63 million, and we’re targeting over 1 million unique coin holders.

 

- Addressing Concerns: Antony acknowledged past missteps, like delaying the team announcement, but reassured everyone of our commitment to transparency and sustainability.

 

- Unproven Technology? Almost all projects start with unproven tech. We’re confident our hybrid approach, integrating existing technologies, will be rock solid by launch.

 

FAQs from the Community

 

- Development Team: We have a strong team of 25 passionate individuals, with source code release expected by the end of August.

 

- Future of BDAG Price: We can’t speculate, but our utility token’s value will depend on network activity.

 

- Institutional Investors: Discussions are ongoing. We’re not just looking for capital but strategic partnerships and sponsorships to fuel growth and propel us even further.

 

The Road Ahead

 

Our long-term vision is ambitious yet achievable: to become a leading Layer 1 blockchain, competing with giants like Ethereum and Solana. We’re also working on partnerships to drive mass adoption, with plans to list on major exchanges like Binance and Coinbase.

 

Security and Innovation

 

We’re prioritizing user security with rigorous audits and fostering innovation with grants and incentives for developers. Our focus on a sustainable, decentralized ecosystem is unwavering.

Why BlockDAG is a Game-Changer

BlockDAG is not just another blockchain project — it’s a hybrid powerhouse. We blend the gold standard of Bitcoin with the speed of DAG technology, delivering unmatched performance every time. Our innovations, like efficient proof-of-work (PoW) and continuous mining reward reduction, set us apart and ensure stability and scalability.



 

The #BlockDAGPhenomenon: From Anonymity to Stardom

The BlockDAG Network started as a regular project in a crowded space but quickly skyrocketed to global recognition. This rise, dubbed the #BlockDAGPhenomenon, is nothing short of extraordinary. How did we do it? Let’s break it down.

 

In just a bit over six months, BlockDAG has gone from being an underdog to a global sensation. We’ve attracted major investors, secured sponsorship from top-tier athletes like global champ UFC Alex Pereirs, and trended across major cities like Tokyo, New York, and London. Our mining app BlockDAG X1 offers passive income, and major publications like Yahoo Finance can’t stop talking about us. The best part? Our presale has already raised nearly $64 million, with prices up by 40% as of yesterday. We’re growing at an unstoppable speed and this is just the beginning!



 

The Future is Always Bright at BlockDAG

Now that we’ve revealed our team and committed to full transparency, the only direction is up. BlockDAG Network is now the top player in the blockchain space. We’re unstoppable, and if you’re not already on board, now’s the time. Don’t miss out on this journey towards the top!

 

Stay tuned for more updates coming tomorrow, and keep following, sharing, and believing. Together, we are BlockDAG Network. Together, we will redefine the future of blockchain.

#BlockDAGPhenomenon #CryptoRevolution #BlockchainFuture #JoinTheMovement #TopCrypto #Unstoppable
