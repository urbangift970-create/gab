An Overview Of Kaspa (KAS)

Kaspa (KAS) was launched in 2021 and is considered one of the fastest and fully scalable Layer-1 solutions in the world. Kaspa is known for its Directed Acyclic Graph (DAG) technology including traditional blockchains that create blocks in sequence. However, Kaspa’s technology allows parallel transaction processing. Hence, Kaspa can deliver speed, scalability, and efficiency. 

 

The project was initiated with a clear goal of overcoming the limitations of traditional blockchains. Kaspa successfully solves the Blockchain trilemma. It strikes a balance between scalability, security, and decentralization. Kaspa is fully decentralized and mineable. This allows for mining at lower hash rates. Kaspa’s standout features include:

 

  • Utilizes DAG technology that enables speed and scalability
  • The GhostDAG Protocol processes parallel blocks into a structure without rejecting them
  • Offers efficient solo mining options at low hash rates 
  • Plans to introduce smart contracts, DeFi, and Layer-2 Applications
