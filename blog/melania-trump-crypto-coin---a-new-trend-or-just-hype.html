Melania Trump Crypto Coin - A New Trend or Just Hype?

Release Date: April 2, 2025

  • A Closer Look at Melania Meme (MELANIA)

  • Melania Price Trends and Market Movements

  • Massive Whale Bet on $MELANIA: High-Leverage Play Revealed

  • $MELANIA Token Supply and Utility

  • What Makes BlockDAG a Smarter Pick Than Melania Crypto Coin?

  • FAQs - Melania Trump Crypto Coin

Melania Trump successfully launched her cryptocurrency on the evening of Donald Trump’s inauguration as the US president, starting his second term. The announcement for the launch of $Melaniacoin  came right after the launch of the $Trump coin. 

 

Both coins have seen a significant surge in price, however, the volatility factor was still high, as always in cryptocurrency trading. In this blog post, we will see and discuss the potential of the Melania Trump crypto coin if it is a new trend in the crypto market or just hype like other coins launching every day. 

A Closer Look at Melania Meme (MELANIA)

Melania Trump is the inspiration behind the official meme currency MELANIA. On January 19, 2025, MKT World introduced it on the Solana blockchain. MKT is a private business founded in Florida in 2021. Crypto traders reacted with enthusiasm when First Lady Trump revealed the token on her social media platforms. According to the information, MELANIA is not associated with any political promotion, office, or government body, nor is it a financial or protection instrument of any type.

 

On the first day of trading, MELANIA's value increased by 21,000%, and buyer demand was so high that the "Jito Labs Block Engine API" declined so badly that it was unable to submit transactions over Solana's network. 

 

Although several users experienced network congestion and difficulties when attempting to obtain the SOL required to convert to MELANIA, Solana did not consider the outcome to be an official outage.

Melania Price Trends and Market Movements

On January 20, 2025, MELANIA began trading at pennies and swiftly jumped to its highest price of $13.64, before dropping following Donald Trump's inauguration. Its price is in the $0.7440 level, having settled around the $0.7099 mark, 95.44% below its ATH.

It might be true that the value of the MELANIA coin can be impacted by the popularity and performance of US President Donald Trump Crypto, but Melania Trump's role as First Lady may make her a powerful influencer who keeps MELANIA in the news. 

 

It is good for potential traders to expect the high price volatility which can pose a substantial risk of loss.

Massive Whale Bet on $MELANIA: High-Leverage Play Revealed

An important market event occurred on March 19, 2025, when a whale trader with an address of '0xf3f4...057c', openly verified by his account 'MELANIA', opened a long position on $MELANIA while using 50x leverage. 

 

According to reports, the stake size was 4.61 million $MELANIA, which converts to a $3.46 million potential value. The whale trader made a calculated move into the $MELANIA token with this activity, which was observed to take place an hour before the tweet.

 

The size of the massive position and the evidence of the whale's identification point to a high positive feeling toward $MELANIA at that particular point in the market. Because such big positions can have significant effects on market dynamics, this event is a clear indicator for investors to closely follow what happens next in price movements and trading volumes of $MELANIA.

$MELANIA Token Supply and Utility

The maximum supply cap of MELANIA is 1 billion tokens. 192.2 million tokens out of 1 billion circulated at the launch of the token. According to the official Melania website, 35% of tokens are specifically reserved for the development team, 20% for the dedicated community, 20% for the treasury, 15% to be distributed among the public, and 10% for the purpose of liquidity. 

 

The team tokens are fully secured for 30 days and have a specified vesting period. The remaining 90% of the team tokens are unlocked gradually through month 13 with 10% of the tokens being unlocked on day 30.

What Makes BlockDAG a Smarter Pick Than Melania Crypto Coin?

When it comes to BlockDAG comparison with Melania or any other new coin in the market, the BDAG always sets itself apart based on long-term strategies focused on sustainability, security, and above all, the hybrid structure of both DAG and blockchain technology. 

 

With its $30M Grants Program, BlockDAG has the potential to take the lead in the blockchain sector. With prizes that range from $5k to $100k in stablecoins and BDAG coins, this project promotes innovative dApps and blockchain technologies.

 

Through this program, developers can use BlockDAG's scalable and quick network to make their ambitious ideas a reality. These awards will contribute to new developments in blockchain technology, whether they concentrate on DeFi, NFTs, or cross-chain solutions. BlockDAG keeps establishing itself as one of the most promising new crypto coins and the main reason for it is the drive for innovation.
