An Overview of AAVE Crypto

Aave was launched in 2017 and was originally known as ETHLend. It was later changed to Aave in 2018. It is a decentralized finance protocol that was designed to facilitate the lending and borrowing of cryptos. Aave facilitates this process through a liquidity pool where lenders deposit digital assets into the pool and borrowers use cryptos as collateral to get loans from this pool.  

 

The AAVE token provides holders with discounted fees and also gives them governance rights. AAVE has successfully become one of the key players in the DeFi ecosystem. The following are some other unique features of the coin:

 

  1. It supports cross-chain compatibility for platforms like Ethereum, Avalanche, Fantom, and Harmony.
  2. AAVE token holders can participate in governance and decision-making.
  3. It offers flash loans where uncollateralized loans must be repaid within the same transaction. 
  4. It provides pools for tangible assets like real estate and cargo. 
  5. Borrowers can switch between fixed and variable interest rates depending on market conditions.
