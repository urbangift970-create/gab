Crypto investors are always looking for cryptocurrency projects that are trending and have the potential for growth for either the short or long-term gains. XDC is one of the growing projects that can provide a significant return on investment. 

 

The hybrid blockchain technology of this project is one of the main factors that make XDC a good investment. In this blog post, you will get to see the XDC price prediction for 2025 and the years ahead of it. 

A Closer Look at XDC Network

Once you understand the XDC Network and price history of the project, it will help you know the price predictions more effectively. XDC Network was first launched in the year 2017 with the main objective of integrating the private and public blockchains. 

 

The decentralization of the infrastructure improves the efficiency of the network, makes it transparent, and removes all the middlemen in between. The XDC Network was founded by Atul Khekade, Ritesh Kakkads, and Karan Bharadwaj, with the headquarters located in Singapore. 

 

The core vision and proficiency of the XDC Network enabled it to overcome all the difficulties in international trading and financial markets, including banks, private financial institutions, buyers, financiers, and suppliers, with significant solutions. 

XDC Price History

The price history of the XDC is necessary to understand before knowing the price predictions, since the forecasts are partly based on the historical price fluctuations and market sentiments. The XDC experienced a peak in April 2018 at the price of $0.0280 but failed to keep the bullish momentum and fell to $0.0240 in the same month. 

XDC encountered another bullish rally in November 2018, peaking at $0.011; however, this time too it failed to sustain the upward trend and dropped to a support level at $0.00067. 

 

The bearish trend of the XDC continued till the first few months of 2021, and finally in August 2021, XDC managed to hit its all-time high at the price of $0.1939. XDC has experienced mixed market sentiments since then and is currently trading at $0.07362. The table mentioned below is a current overview of the XDC price and ranking as of April 15, 2025.

XDC Network Price Prediction

There are many factors involved in impacting the price of the XDC; this is why we are going to see the XDC price forecast from both a bullish and a bearish point of view to understand the real potential of price movements.

 

XDC Price Prediction: A Bullish Outlook

 

In the highly uncertain market, and the scenario of crypto market capitalization of $3 trillion, XDC will maintain its stance, which shows that with the positive crypto market conditions, XDC can experience a bullish rally in the upcoming years. If we see the higher bullish scenario, the crypto market cap at $10 trillion can dramatically increase the value of the XDC more than 7 times, with the anticipated price of $0.39.

 

XDC Price Forecast: A Bearish Outlook

 

Considering the bearish trend situations and negative conditions of the crypto market, along with the strong competition, the price of XDC can fall significantly. In such a case, despite the fact that the overall crypto market capitalization reaches $3 trillion, XDC is expected to trade around $0.059 in the short term and exceed the price target of $0.19 by 2030. 

Make sure to understand that these bullish and bearish scenarios can change based on market sentiments, economic conditions, and the overall crypto market, so it shouldn’t be taken as financial advice. 

 

You must conduct thorough research on the crypto market and the project you are planning to invest in.

Will XDC Hit $100 Anytime Soon?

Based on current market trends, it is almost impossible for the XDC price to reach $100 before 2040. While it's hard to predict the exact future price of any cryptocurrency or other digital asset, it seems that XDC is not going to hit this mark in the near term or even in the coming 20 years.

 

However, the cryptocurrency market is very uncertain and hard to predict due to various factors. Is XDC a Good Investment In 2025?

XDC is a blockchain technology with the vision to change global trade finance. Its partnerships, like the one with SBI Holdings, show how XDC can grow internationally and work in different sectors. The upcoming XDC 2.0 upgrade will improve the network's speed and security. 

 

These improvements will help XDC manage more transactions and attract developers, leading to more new projects and ideas on its platform.

 

All the indicators, technical, and price analysis suggest a positive outlook for the XDC for 2025 and the next 10 to 20 years. However, evaluating the risk factor, market sentiments, economic conditions, and the overall crypto market situation is highly recommended and necessary. 

 

The price predictions can change based on these factors. Understanding the relevant conditions and a thorough research can help you to make sound investment decisions and mitigate the risk factor involved. 

Why BlockDAG is a Smarter Alternative to Traditional Blockchain?

The cryptocurrency market is going through major changes as key players attract both experienced and new investors. One significant development is BlockDAG, an innovative alternative to traditional blockchain technology. BlockDAG recently raised $213 million in presales, marking an important step in its growth as a strong competitor in the blockchain space.

 

BlockDAG, which is known for its Distributed Acyclic Graph structure, has gained attention due to its distinctive structure. It overcomes most of the limitations of standard blockchains. Unlike traditional blockchains that connect transactions in a straight line, BlockDAG allows more than one branch to be added at the same time. 

 

This parallel processing improves scalability and increases transaction speed. These improvements make BlockDAG a potential game-changer for industries that need fast and efficient transactions.

 

The recent $213 million ongoing crypto presale 2025 shows that investors are confident in BlockDAG’s future. They are excited about the efficiency of its design, which can provide faster and cheaper transactions compared to established blockchain networks. This significant investment will be important for BlockDAG as it works to expand its market reach and speed up its development.
