Countdown to Launch: Final Development Updates Before the Big Reveal!

This development release brings exciting updates as we edge closer to the highly anticipated launch in the fourth week of March! With critical progress across our ecosystem, we are fine-tuning every aspect to ensure a smooth and seamless experience for our users. 
Get ready for a major milestone in our journey!

Key Highlights

  • Testnet Name Finalized: The name for our testnet has been decided! However, to keep the excitement high, it will be revealed only at the official launch stay tuned!
  • Blockchain Advancements: A brand-new network configuration has been set up, and our developers have started rigorous unit testing.
  • Full Ecosystem Testing in Progress: All associated platforms have been deployed on the server, and we have initiated end-to-end ecosystem testing.
  • Parallel Bug Fixing: While the QA phase is underway, our stack developers are working tirelessly to squash any bugs.
  • Explorer Enhancements: Only minor design fixes remain before we finalize the Explorer.
  • dApp Investigation: We identified an issue where users couldn't see their transactions. Our team is actively working to resolve this.
  • X1 Miner Rebranding: Discussions have begun on revamping the X1 Miner application, including its positioning and updates for Android and iOS.
  • Second Phase Planning: Conversations are underway to define the scope and timelines for the next phase of the release.

Why It Matters

As we move towards the official launch, these updates mark significant progress in building a robust, high-performance, and user-friendly ecosystem. The enhancements and fixes being implemented now will set the stage for a seamless and efficient experience for our users.

Blockchain Updates

  • The new network configuration has been successfully set up and is undergoing thorough unit testing to ensure stability and performance.
  • The entire ecosystem has been deployed on this upgraded server, enabling an extensive QA process to identify and resolve any issues before the launch.
  • While QA testing continues, our developers are working in parallel to address bugs, optimizing the network for peak efficiency.

Explorer Updates

  • We are in the final polishing phase, with only minor design tweaks left.
  • No major structural changes are required, as the core functionalities are already in place and performing well.
  • We are ensuring a visually appealing and smooth user experience before the final rollout.

dApp Updates

  • A crucial issue has been identified where users were unable to view their past transactions.
  • Our team is actively investigating the root cause and collaborating closely to implement a quick and effective fix.
  • This fix is a top priority to ensure a seamless experience for all users interacting with the dApp.

X1 Miner App Updates

  • Discussions have commenced on the rebranding of the X1 Miner application, focusing on both aesthetics and functionality.
  • Our Android and iOS teams are brainstorming the best approach to align the app with our ecosystem's vision and branding.
  • The rebranding strategy will bring a fresh and enhanced experience for miners, making it even more intuitive and user-friendly.

IDE Updates

  • We are actively planning the second phase of the release, evaluating the features and improvements that should be included.
  • Discussions are focused on setting realistic but ambitious timelines to ensure a swift and effective rollout.

Fixed Issues

  • Ongoing bug fixes in the blockchain ecosystem to enhance performance and reliability.
  • Minor UI and UX enhancements in the Explorer for a more seamless user experience.

Open Issues

  • Investigating and resolving the transaction visibility issue in the DApp.
  • Further strategizing the X1 Miner rebranding and implementation plan.

Next Steps

  • Complete full-scale ecosystem testing and resolve any remaining issues to ensure a flawless launch.
  • Finalize the rebranding roadmap for the X1 Miner application, aligning it with our long-term vision.
  • Outline the next phase of development, incorporating community feedback and evolving platform requirements.
  • Keep the community engaged and excited as we approach the fourth week of March launch!

Feedback & Support

We value your input! If you encounter issues, have suggestions, or want to share your excitement with us, reach out at [email protected]. Your feedback helps us build a better ecosystem for everyone.

Conclusion

The momentum is building, and we are getting closer to a major milestone, the official launch in the fourth week of March! Every update, every fix, and every discussion we have is bringing us one step closer to a seamless and powerful ecosystem that will serve as the foundation for a decentralized future.
 

With full-scale ecosystem testing in progress, our team is working around the clock to ensure that every component functions flawlessly. The excitement among our developers, designers, and community members is at an all-time high, and we are committed to delivering an exceptional experience for everyone involved.
 

The upcoming launch will not only mark the debut of our network but also unveil the highly anticipated testnet name, a symbol of our vision and progress. As we continue our final refinements, we invite the community to stay engaged, share feedback, and be a part of this transformative journey. The best is yet to come, brace yourself for an exciting new chapter!
