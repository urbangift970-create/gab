Exciting Authentication Upgrades Coming to the X1 Miner App!

Hello BlockDAG Community!

We’re thrilled to unveil a major leap forward in the X1 Miner app’s evolution! Our team is hard at work developing a brand-new, state-of-the-art authentication system that will redefine security, usability, and convenience for our users. These updates are designed to enhance your mining experience on BlockDAG, bringing you faster access, top-tier security, and a personalized touch that sets us apart from the rest.
Although these features are still in development, we’ve completed the initial designs and are simultaneously building out the backend to bring them to life. Here’s a sneak peek into what’s coming and how we’re making it happen!

Designing the Next Level of Authentication

Our design team has been crafting an intuitive and visually engaging user experience for the new authentication system. These designs prioritize simplicity, clarity, and ease of use while maintaining a sleek and modern aesthetic.
Here's what you can expect from the upcoming user flows:User Signup: Designed for Security and Simplicity
The signup process begins with validating your phone number via OTP, ensuring only genuine users can create accounts. 

Following this, you’ll be guided through:

  • Personalization: Enter your username, first name, and last name.
  • Secure Login Setup: Create a robust password for primary access.
  • Optional Biometric Authentication: On supported devices, enable fingerprint or facial recognition for seamless, password-free logins.
  • Email Integration: Add an optional email for account recovery, verified via OTP to strengthen security.


User Login: Built for Speed and Accessibility
The login flow adapts to your preferences. Whether you prefer password-based access or biometric authentication, our design ensures a frictionless experience. Forgot your password? The system intuitively redirects you to a recovery flow that’s just as user-friendly.

Biometric Authentication: Privacy-First Convenience
For users on supported devices, biometric authentication ensures instant access while keeping your data secure. All biometric information remains on your device, eliminating privacy concerns.

Session Management: Keep Your Account Complete
The designs also include intelligent prompts during active sessions to fill in any missing details like email or password. This keeps your account fully secure and operational without interrupting your workflow.

Building the Backend: Engineering for Excellence

While the design team is perfecting the user interface, our backend engineers are laying the groundwork to support these advanced features.

Here’s how the development is shaping up:

User Authentication System

  • Phone Number Validation: A robust OTP system ensures only valid users can sign up or log in.
  • Biometric Integration: Backend mechanisms support secure toggling for fingerprint and facial recognition. All sensitive data remains stored on the user’s device for privacy.
  • Error Handling: Built-in logic to guide users through incorrect inputs (e.g., invalid OTPs or passwords), making the process smooth and frustration-free.


Password Recovery and Account Security

  • Secure Password Reset: OTP-based validation ensures only legitimate users can reset passwords.
  • Recovery Enhancements: Additional safeguards, such as verified email addresses, provide multiple layers of protection for account recovery.


Session and Detail Management

  • Active Session Monitoring: Ensures email verification is complete before starting mining sessions.
  • Dynamic Prompts: Back-end logic dynamically identifies missing details and requests updates without disrupting user activity.

Feature Highlights: What to Look Forward To

Our new authentication system combines cutting-edge security measures with seamless workflows to ensure that mining on BlockDAG remains your top priority while we take care of everything else. 
Here’s a breakdown of what you can expect:

User Signup Process: The Ultimate Verification!
Get ready to say goodbye to fake accounts and unnecessary clutter in your mining world! The new signup process begins with a phone number validation step that ensures only real, active users get through the door. With the added layer of OTP-based confirmation, you can be sure that your account is truly yours from the very start. Once verified, you’ll enter your personal details (username, first name, last name) to customize your experience, followed by a secure password creation for your primary login. But here’s where it gets even better—if your device supports it, you can opt into biometric authentication like fingerprint or facial recognition for ultra-fast, password-free logins! And don’t worry—no biometric data is stored on the backend, keeping your privacy at the forefront. Oh, and if you’re looking for added convenience, you can also add an email address for easy account recovery, which will be verified via a separate OTP. It’s a full-circle security experience tailored just for you!

User Login Flow: Fast, Secure, and Flexible!
Logging in to your account has never been easier! Simply enter your registered phone number, and you’re on your way. If you’ve set a password, you’ll be prompted to enter it. But for those who’ve enabled biometric authentication, the system will automatically recognize you, letting you skip the password entry and get straight to mining! No password? No problem! OTP-based logins via phone or email are here to ensure that you’re always just a few clicks away from accessing your account. We’ve built intelligent error handling into the system, so if anything goes wrong (like an incorrect password or OTP), clear and concise prompts will guide you through the process, keeping things smooth and user-friendly.

Forgot Password Flow: Secure Recovery, Hassle-Free!
We’ve made the password recovery process so simple—and safe—that you’ll wonder why you ever worried about losing access in the first place! It all starts with phone number verification, followed by setting up a new, secure password. We add an extra layer of security by sending an OTP to your email or phone to confirm your identity before you’re allowed to finalize the change. Once you’re back in, we’ll even invite you to set up biometric authentication for even faster future access!

Biometric Authentication: The Future of Login Is Here!
For those who want the fastest, most convenient login experience, biometric authentication is the way to go. Available only on supported devices, you can use fingerprint or facial recognition to log in instantly, skipping the password step altogether. The best part? All biometric data stays securely stored on your device, ensuring maximum privacy—with no sensitive data being stored on our servers. Your privacy and security are our top priorities, and this feature combines the best of both worlds: speed and security.

Managing Active Sessions: Effortless and Secure
We’re taking security even further by adding robust session management. Users without a verified email address will be prompted to complete email verification before starting any mining sessions, making sure you can always recover your account if needed. Plus, the system will dynamically prompt you to fill in any missing details (like your name, password, or email) during active sessions, ensuring that you can stay fully connected and active without interruptions.

Why This Upgrade Matters

These updates are more than just an upgrade—they’re a commitment to our community. 
We’re creating a system that’s:

  • Secure: Every step, from signup to session management, prioritizes the safety of your account.
  • Efficient: Whether it’s biometric login or OTP-based recovery, we’re making access faster and hassle-free.
  • User-Centric: With personalized features like username setup and email recovery, your experience is truly tailored to your needs.
  • Transparent: Clear instructions and real-time feedback ensure a smooth journey through every interaction.

What’s Next?

The authentication redesign is just the beginning! As we finalize these features, we’ll continue refining both the designs and backend infrastructure to ensure everything works seamlessly.

Thank you for being a part of the BlockDAG journey—we’re building this future together!
