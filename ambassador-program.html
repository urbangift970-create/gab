BlockDAG Ambassador Program: Join the Movement

The BlockDAG Ambassador Program is your gateway to becoming an integral part of a fast-growing blockchain ecosystem.

Whether you’re a developer, content creator, community leader, or blockchain enthusiast, this program is designed to reward and empower those who are passionate about decentralization, innovation, and building a strong global network.

BlockDAG Ambassador Program: Join the Movement

Why Become a BlockDAG Ambassador?

Earn BDAG Tokens & Exclusive Rewards

Active ambassadors receive BDAG token incentives, exclusive merchandise, and access to special opportunities.

Grow Your Influence

Become a recognized leader in the BlockDAG ecosystem, gain visibility, and expand your professional network.

Access to Private Community Channels

Join exclusive Discord and Telegram groups where Ambassadors discuss strategies, get early updates, and collaborate on initiatives.

Opportunities for Growth

Top Ambassadors can move into leadership roles, host official events, and even represent BlockDAG at global conferences.

Hands-on Experience in Blockchain & Web3

Learn directly from industry experts and level up your skills.

Roles & Responsibilities

BlockDAG Ambassadors are divided into different tiers, each with increasing levels of responsibility and rewards:

BlockDAG Champion

(Senior-Level)

Lead regional communities, acting as the primary point of contact for BlockDAG in your region.

Organize larger-scale events, hackathons, and in-person workshops.

Represent BlockDAG at global blockchain conferences and partner events.

Provide mentorship to new Ambassadors and help drive new ecosystem initiatives.

BlockDAG Advocate

(Intermediate-Level)

Organize and host local meetups, study groups, or online sessions.

Create educational content about BlockDAG (tutorials, guides, explainer videos).

Onboard new developers and users into the ecosystem.

Assist in moderating community channels and engaging with new members.

BlockDAG Explorer

(Entry-Level)

Engage in discussions and spread awareness on social media.

Create and share content (tweets, blog posts, videos, memes).

Participate in online events, webinars, and AMAs.

Compensation & Rewards

Primary Compensation

BDAG Tokens for contributions, initiatives, and milestones.

Bonus Incentives

Select Ambassadors may receive USDT stipends based on exceptional contributions.

Event Access & Perks

Gain access to exclusive BlockDAG events, conferences, and private networking sessions.

Exclusive Merchandise & Badges

Earn Ambassador-only swag, NFTs, and digital credentials.
