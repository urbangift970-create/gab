BlockDAG Security Commitment

At BlockDAG, security is at the core of everything we do.

We understand that trust is earned through transparency and rigorous testing, and our multi-layered security strategy reflects our dedication to protecting our blockchain ecosystem and its users.

STAGE ONE

Treasury & Vesting Smart Contract Audit

We have implemented a comprehensive three-level approach to ensure the utmost security for our primary treasury and vesting smart contract:

Internal Audit
Completed

Our in-house team conducted an exhaustive review using advanced code analysis tools, extensive unit tests, and integration tests to identify and address potential vulnerabilities.

Looking ahead

Comprehensive
Blockchain Audit

Following the beta release of our testnet, we will initiate a full-scale, third-party audit of the entire BlockDAG blockchain. This audit will cover critical components, including:

Protocol & Consensus Mechanism Security

Ensuring our core blockchain protocols and consensus algorithms are resilient against attacks.

Network & Node Security

Evaluating the integrity and robustness of our network architecture and node infrastructure.

Smart Contract Security

Extending our rigorous audits across all deployed contracts.

Cryptographic Security

Assessing the strength and implementation of our cryptographic techniques.

Blockchain Data & Storage Security

Verifying that data integrity and storage solutions remain secure and reliable.

DeFi & Tokenomics Security

Reviewing our decentralized finance frameworks and token economic models for potential risks.

Governance & Upgradeability Risks

Analyzing the mechanisms for blockchain governance and future upgrades.

Interoperability & Cross-Chain Security

Ensuring secure interactions with other chains and external systems.

Performance & Scalability Testing

Stress-testing our blockchain to confirm its performance and scalability under load.

Incident Response & Monitoring

Implementing robust strategies for rapid detection, response, and mitigation of potential security incidents.

COMMUNITY ENGAGEMENT

Bug Bounty Program

In addition to our extensive audit processes, we recognize that community participation is vital for achieving a truly secure ecosystem. To further bolster our defenses, we will launch a Bug Bounty Program immediately following our primordial testnet release. This initiative will:

Incentivize Ethical Hacking

Reward security researchers and ethical hackers for identifying and reporting vulnerabilities.

Enhance Transparency

Engage the global security community in continuous testing, ensuring that even the most elusive issues are identified and addressed.

Accelerate Resolution

Enable us to promptly patch any vulnerabilities with insights provided directly by the community.

Our commitment to security is a collaborative effort, and the Bug Bounty Program is a key component of our strategy to build a resilient and trustworthy blockchain.

Organizational Security

Beyond technical audits and community-driven security measures, BlockDAG has instituted comprehensive organizational security protocols. In the lead-up to and following our listing, we enforce strict processes to protect sensitive information—ensuring the integrity of our investor list and adherence to a meticulously structured vesting schedule.

Our robust financial controls include regular financial audits and multi-person approval processes to safeguard the treasury of both fiat and crypto assets. Additionally, BlockDAG has implemented the MPC platform ForDeFi, which leverages advanced cryptographic techniques and distributed key management to secure our on-chain assets.

Our Promise

At BlockDAG, our commitment to security is unwavering. Through continuous audits, a proactive bug bounty program, and state-of-the-art security practices, we strive to build a blockchain ecosystem that is both innovative and secure.
